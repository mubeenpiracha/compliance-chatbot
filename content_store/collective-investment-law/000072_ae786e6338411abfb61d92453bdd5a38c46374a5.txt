A person does not cease to be independent for the purposes of this Article merely by virtue of being appointed as a person providing the oversight function for a Fund.

The DFSA may, by Rules, prescribe when a legal entity appointed as the person providing the oversight function to a Fund may or may not meet the independence criteria for the purposes of this Article.
