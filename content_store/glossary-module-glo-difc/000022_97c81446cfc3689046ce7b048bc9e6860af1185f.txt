GLOSSARY (GLO)

Definition

(a) ensures the performance of open contracts relating to Investments, made on a facility for trading Investments; and

(b) does so by interposing between counterparties to such contracts by becoming the buyer to every seller, or the seller to every buyer.

In relation to:

(a)

an Authorised Firm, the meaning given to that term under GEN Rule 2.13.1(3); and

(b)

in the case of an Authorised Market Institution, the meaning given to that term in GEN Rule 2.18.1(5).

Has the meaning given in GEN Rule A2.2.1(d).

Any Fee or charge made to a Client in connection with Investment Business, whether levied by the Authorised Firm or any other Person, including a Mark-up or Mark- down.

The individual appointed to the office of Chief Executive of the DFSA by the Board.
