Multilateral Trading Facility

GLOSSARY (GLO)

Definition

to operate a Multilateral Trading Facility.

Pursuant to GEN Rule 2.22.1(a), a system which brings together multiple third party buying and selling interests in Investments or Crypto Tokens, in accordance with its non-discretionary rules, in a way that results in a contract in respect of such Investments or Crypto Tokens.
