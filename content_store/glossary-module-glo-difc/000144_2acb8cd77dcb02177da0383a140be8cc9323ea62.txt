Definition

A report referred to in GEN Rule 8.6.1(e).

Means a Client Crypto Token held or to be held for safekeeping by an Authorised Firm or Third Party Agent.

Means a Client Investment held or to be held for safekeeping by an Authorised Firm or Third Party Agent.
