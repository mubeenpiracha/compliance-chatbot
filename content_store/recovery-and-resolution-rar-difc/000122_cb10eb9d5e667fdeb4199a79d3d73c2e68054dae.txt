Protection for security arrangements

The DFSA shall not, in respect of liabilities secured under the collateral arrangements referred to in Rule 3.6.2(2)(a) that are not title transfer collateral arrangements:

(a)

transfer assets against which a liability is secured under any such arrangement, unless that liability and the benefit of the security are also transferred;

(b)

transfer a secured liability, unless the benefit of the security is also transferred;

(c)

transfer the benefit of the security under any such arrangement, unless the secured liability is also transferred; or

(d) modify or terminate such an arrangement through the exercise of ancillary powers, if the effect of that modification or termination is that the liability ceases to be secured.
