The Trust Deed shall:

(a)

meet all the requirements that apply in respect of the Constitution of a Fund under the Collective Investment Law 2010 and the rules made for the purposes of that law;

(b)

set out clearly whether the Trustee is to provide the oversight function relating to the Investment Trust;

(c)

confer on the Trustee all the powers that are necessary for the Trustee to discharge all its duties and perform all its functions under this Law and the Collective Investment Law 2010 and the rules made for the purposes of those laws; and

(d)

not contain any provision which conflicts with the requirements in the Collective Investment Law 2010 and rules made for the purposes of that law.
