Attribution of contracts to a fund

3.3.1 All contracts of Long-Term Insurance effected by either an ADGM Incorporated Insurer or an Insurer that is not an ADGM Incorporated Insurer through an establishment in ADGM must be attributed to a Long-Term Insurance Fund.

3.3.2 An Insurer may only attribute Contracts of Insurance in General Insurance Class 1 or

Class 2 to a Long-Term Insurance Fund.

3.4

Segregation of assets and liabilities

3.4.1 An Insurer may at any time attribute any of its assets to a Long-Term Insurance Fund that

were not previously attributed to such a Long-Term Insurance Fund.
