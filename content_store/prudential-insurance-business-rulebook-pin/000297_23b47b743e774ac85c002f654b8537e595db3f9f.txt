A5.7.2 Owners' Equity in a Takaful Insurer other than amounts referred to in Rule A5.7.1(c) must

be classified as hybrid capital for the purposes of this section if:

(a)

under the constitutional documents of the Insurer or the terms of insurance contracts or both, participation in the surpluses and losses of Takaful business is limited to the policyholders of the Insurer; and

(b)

the Owners' Equity is available for loan to the Insurance Fund of the Insurer.

A5.7.3 Hybrid cellular capital having a term to maturity of less than five years may only be

included in base cellular capital with the consent of the Regulator.
