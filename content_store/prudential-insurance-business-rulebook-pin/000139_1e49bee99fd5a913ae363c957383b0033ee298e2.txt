Significant transactions other than group transactions

8.5.1

(1)

An ADGM Incorporated Insurer must not enter into a transaction of the type described in this Rule unless the directors of the Insurer are satisfied following reasonable enquiry that the transaction is at arms-length and does not adversely affect the interests of policyholders. The transactions to be considered are:

(a)

a sale, purchase, exchange, loan or extension of credit, guarantee or investment where the counterparty is a Person Related to the Insurer and the amount of the transaction equals or exceeds 3% of the Insurer's surplus as at the end of the financial year immediately preceding the transaction;
