The requirement for an actuarial report on General Insurance Business

7.2.1 Subject to Rule 7.2.2, this section applies to Insurers conducting General Insurance

Business.

7.2.2 Where an Insurer attributes General Insurance Business to a Long-Term Insurance Fund

in accordance with Rule 3.3.2, this section does not apply to that business.

7.2.3 Every Insurer must provide to the Regulator as at each reporting date a written report relating to its General Insurance Business, prepared by an Actuary who has the qualifications set out in Rule 7.5.
