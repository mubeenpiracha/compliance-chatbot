TRANSITIONAL RULES

10.1 Endorsements to hold client assets and insurance monies

Interpretation

10.1.1 In this section:

“commencement date” means the day on which Rule-Making Instrument No 166 of 2016 comes into force;

“endorsement” means an endorsement under:

(a)

Rule 2.2.10A permitting an Authorised Firm to hold or control Client Assets; or

(b)

Rule 2.2.10B permitting an Authorised Firm to hold Insurance Monies;

“transitional period” means the period starting on the commencement date and ending six months after that day.
