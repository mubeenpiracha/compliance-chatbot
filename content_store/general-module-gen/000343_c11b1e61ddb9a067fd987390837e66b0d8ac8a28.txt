GENERAL PROVISIONS

6.1 Application

6.1.1

(1)

Sections 6.1, 6.2, 6.3, 6.9 and 6.10 apply to every Person to whom any provision in the Rulebook applies.

(2)

Section 6.4 applies to every Authorised Person.

(3)

Sections 6.5 and 6.6 apply to every Authorised Firm, Authorised Market Institution and Person who has submitted an application for authorisation to carry on one or more Financial Services.

(4)

Section 6.7 applies to any Person who has been affected by the activities of the DFSA.
