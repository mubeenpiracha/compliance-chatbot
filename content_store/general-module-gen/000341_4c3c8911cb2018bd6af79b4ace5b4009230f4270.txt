Notification

5.5.19 An Authorised Person must notify the DFSA as soon as reasonably practicable, and in any event no later than 72 hours, after it becomes aware, or has information which reasonably suggests, that a material Cyber Incident has occurred, using the appropriate form available on the DFSA electronic portal.
