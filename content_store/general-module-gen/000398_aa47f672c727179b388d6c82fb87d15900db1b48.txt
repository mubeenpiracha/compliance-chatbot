Guidance

In considering whether individuals have sufficient knowledge of relevant anti money laundering requirements, the DFSA may be satisfied where the individual can demonstrate receipt of appropriate training specifically relevant to such requirements.

7.6.14 An Authorised Firm must establish and maintain systems and controls which

will enable it to comply with Rules 7.6.6 to 7.6.9.

7.6.15 (1)

An Authorised Firm must keep records of the assessment process undertaken for each individual under this chapter.

(2)

These records must be kept for a minimum of six years from the date of the assessment.
