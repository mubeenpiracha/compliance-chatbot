In this Rule:

(a)

“head office” means the principal place where an Authorised Person carries on:

(i)

the day-to-day management and control of its business, wherever that business may be conducted; and

(ii)

the activities for which it is authorised by the DFSA; and

(b)

“registered office” has the meaning given in the Companies Law, Limited Partnership Law 2004 or General Partnership Law 2006, as applicable.
