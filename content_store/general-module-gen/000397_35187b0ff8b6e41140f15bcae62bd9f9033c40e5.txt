Systems and controls

7.6.12 An Authorised Firm must have appropriate arrangements in place to ensure that an individual assessed as being competent under Rule 7.6.6 maintains his competence.

7.6.13 An Authorised Firm must ensure, in the case of individuals seeking to perform the Licensed Functions of Senior Executive Officer, Money Laundering Reporting Officer, or Compliance Officer, that such individuals are able to demonstrate sufficient knowledge of relevant anti money laundering requirements.
