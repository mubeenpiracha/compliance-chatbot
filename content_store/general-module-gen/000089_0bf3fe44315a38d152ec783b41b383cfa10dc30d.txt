Exclusions

2.11.2 A Person does not Advise on Financial Products by giving advice in any newspaper, journal, magazine, broadcast service or similar service in any medium if the principal purpose of the publication or service, taken as a whole, is neither:

(a)

that of giving advice of the kind mentioned in Rule 2.11.1; nor

(b)

that of leading or enabling Persons to buy, sell, subscribe for or underwrite a particular financial product of the kind in Rule 2.11.1(4).
