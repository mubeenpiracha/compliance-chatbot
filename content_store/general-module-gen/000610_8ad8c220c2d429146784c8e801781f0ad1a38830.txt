APP6

GENERAL (GEN)

INVESTMENT TOKENS

Guidance

Purpose of this Guidance

1.

A Person who carries on certain activities relating to Investment Tokens, in or from the DIFC, will require DFSA approval or authorisation, or will need to comply with applicable requirements in DFSA administered laws or Rules. Those activities may include:

a.

carrying on a Financial Service (as defined in GEN section 2.2.) which relates to an Investment Token (such as operating a facility on which Investment Tokens are traded or cleared, or giving advice on, or arranging deals in such Investment Tokens);
