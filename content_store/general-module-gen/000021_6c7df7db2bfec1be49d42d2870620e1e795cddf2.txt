FINANCIAL SERVICES

2.1 Application

2.1.1 This chapter applies to every Person to whom the Regulatory Law applies,

and to the same extent in relation to every such Person as that law.

2.2 Financial Service activities

2.2.1 An activity constitutes a Financial Service under the Regulatory Law and

these Rules where:

(a)

it is an activity specified in Rule 2.2.2; and

(b)

such activity is carried on by way of business in the manner described in section 2.3.
