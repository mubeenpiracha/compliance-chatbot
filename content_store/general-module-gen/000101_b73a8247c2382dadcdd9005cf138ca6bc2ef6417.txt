11.10.3 A Domestic Firm must provide the DFSA with reasonable advance notice of the establishment or closure of a branch office anywhere in the world from which it carries on financial services.

11.10.4 When giving notice under Rule 11.10.3 in relation to the establishment of a branch, a Domestic Firm must at the same time submit to the DFSA a detailed business plan in relation to the activities of the proposed branch.

11.10.5 (1)

The DFSA may object to the establishment by a Domestic Firm of a branch office elsewhere in the world.
