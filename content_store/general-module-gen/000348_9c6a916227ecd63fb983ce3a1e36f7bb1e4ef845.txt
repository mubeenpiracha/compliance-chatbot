6.4 Disclosure of regulatory status

6.4.1 An Authorised Person must not misrepresent its status expressly or by

implication.

6.4.2

(1)

An Authorised Person must take reasonable care to ensure that every key business document which is in connection with the Authorised Person carrying on a Financial Service in or from the DIFC includes one of the disclosures under this Rule.
