6.10 Manner of market disclosure

6.10.1 Where a Reporting Entity of a Listed Fund is required to make market disclosure of information pursuant to a provision in this chapter, such information must be disclosed to the market in accordance with the requirements in Section 4.7.

6.10.2 A Reporting Entity of a Listed Fund must retain on its website all information that has

been disclosed to the market for a period of one year following publication.
