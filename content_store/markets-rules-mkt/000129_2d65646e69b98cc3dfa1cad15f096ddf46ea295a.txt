4.2 Disclosure of inside information

Timely disclosure

4.2.1

(1)

A Reporting Entity must make timely disclosure of Inside Information in accordance with the requirements in this section.

(2)

A Reporting Entity must ensure that the disclosure it makes pursuant to (1) is not misleading, false or deceptive and does not omit anything likely to affect the import of the information.

(3)

For the purposes of complying with the requirement in (1), the Reporting Entity must, subject to Rule 4.2.3 and 4.2.4, make disclosure as soon as possible and in the manner specified in Rule 4.7.1.
