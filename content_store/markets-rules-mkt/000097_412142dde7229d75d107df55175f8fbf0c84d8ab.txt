GOVERNANCE OF REPORTING ENTITIES

Guidance

Governance requirements set out under this Part are designed for the purposes of Article 39 of the Law.

3.1 Application

3.1.1

(1)

This chapter applies to every Reporting Entity except where a narrower application is provided in respect of any particular class of Securities.

(2)

This chapter does not apply to a Reporting Entity of a Listed Fund or an ATS Traded Fund.

Guidance

See Chapter 6 for the governance requirements applicable to Reporting Entities of Listed Funds and ATS Traded Funds.
