3.2 Corporate governance principles

Application

3.2.1 This section applies to a Reporting Entity in respect of Shares, and the Board of

Directors (“the Board”) of such a Reporting Entity.

Corporate governance principles

3.2.2 Pursuant to Article 39(2)(a) of the Law, the principles in Rules 3.2.3 to 3.2.9 are

hereby prescribed as “the Corporate Governance Principles”.
