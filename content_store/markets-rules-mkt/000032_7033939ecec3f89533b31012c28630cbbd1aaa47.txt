1.3.1 A reference in this MKT module to:

(a)

“the Law”, is a reference to the Markets Law 2012;

(b)

“this module”, is a reference to this MKT module; and

(c)

“Rules”, except where otherwise provided, is a reference to the Rules in this module.

1.3.2 Where a Reporting Entity is referred to in this module as a Reporting Entity in respect of a specified class of Securities, it is a reference to a Person who has become a Reporting Entity by:

(a)

making an Offer of Securities to the Public;

(b)

having Securities admitted to trading on an Authorised Market Institution; or

(c)

having Security Tokens admitted to trading on an Alternative Trading System,

of that particular specified class of Securities.
