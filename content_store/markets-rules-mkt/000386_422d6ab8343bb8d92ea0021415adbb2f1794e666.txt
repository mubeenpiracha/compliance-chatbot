Concurrent offers by directors of the issuer (a)

If one or more members of the Board of Directors of the issuer are offering their Shares under the same Prospectus:

(i) the identity of each member making such offers;

(ii)

the number of Shares each such Person is offering; and

(iii)

the proportion of the holding of the member that those Shares represent.

(b)

If no member of the Board is offering his Shares, a statement to that effect.
