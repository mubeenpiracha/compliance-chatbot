3.3. Group Structure

If the issuer is a member of a Group, information about the issuer’s Group including:

(a)

identity of all the members of the Group;

(b)

a brief description of the Group explaining the issuer’s position within the Group;

(c)

the identity of the ultimate Holding Company of the issuer and where it is domiciled; and

(d)

a list of significant Subsidiaries of the issuer, including name, country of incorporation or domicile, proportion of ownership interest and, if different, proportion of voting power or other form of control held.
