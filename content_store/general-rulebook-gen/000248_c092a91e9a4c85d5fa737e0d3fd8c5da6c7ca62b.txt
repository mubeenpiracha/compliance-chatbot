This Rule applies to Insurers that are Protected Cell Companies or Incorporated Cell

Companies.

Guidance

1.

An Insurer that is a Protected Cell Company is a company incorporated as, or converted into, a Protected Cell Company in accordance with the provisions of the Companies Regulations.

2.

An Insurer that is an Incorporated Cell Company is a company incorporated as, or converted into, an Incorporated Cell Company in accordance with the provisions of the Companies Regulations.
