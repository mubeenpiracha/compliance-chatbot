Guidance

1.

A Credit Rating Agency is not subject to PRU including capital requirements.

2.

A Fund Manager of a Venture Capital Fund is not subject to PRU except 6.12 (professional indemnity insurance).

3.

However both are required, pursuant to GEN Rules 2.2.4 and 5.2.8, to have and maintain adequate financial resources to manage its affairs prudently and soundly.
