Notification

An Authorised Person must notify the Regulator immediately, and in any event no later than 24 hours, after it becomes aware or has information which reasonably suggests that a material Cyber Incident has occurred. Notification to the Regulator should use the prescribed incident reporting form available on the relevant electronic portal.
