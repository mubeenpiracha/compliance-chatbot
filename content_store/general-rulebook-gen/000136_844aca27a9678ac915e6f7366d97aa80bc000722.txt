Consideration and assessment of applications

In order to become authorised to carry on one or more Regulated Activities, the applicant must demonstrate to the satisfaction of the Regulator that it:

(1)

has adequate and appropriate resources, including financial resources;

(2)

is fit and proper;

(3)

is capable of being effectively supervised; and

(4)

has adequate compliance arrangements, including policies and procedures, that will enable it to comply with all the applicable legal requirements, including the Rules.
