Remuneration structure and strategies

(1)

The Governing Body of an Authorised Person must ensure that the Remuneration structure and strategy of that Authorised Person:

(a)

are consistent with the business objectives and strategies and the identified risk parameters within which the Authorised Person's business is to be conducted;

(b)
