Guidance

1.

See Rules 8.8.2 and 8.8.3 for the circumstances in which a Person becomes a Controller of an Authorised Person.

2.

The Regulator recognises that Authorised Persons acting as Investment Managers may have difficulties in complying with any prior notification requirements in these Rules as a result of acquiring or disposing of listed Shares in the course of that Fund management activity. To ameliorate these difficulties, the Regulator may accept pre-notification of proposed changes in control and may grant approval of such changes for a period lasting up to a year.
