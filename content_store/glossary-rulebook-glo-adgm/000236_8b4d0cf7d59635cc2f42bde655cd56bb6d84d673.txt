Virtual Asset

Has the meaning given in section 258 of FSMR.

Waiver

Means, in relation to GEN 8.2, a Direction given under section 9 of FSMR that one or more Rules made by the Regulator are not to apply to an Authorised Person, a Recognised Body, a Remote Body, a Remote Member, a Foreign Fund Manager, a Person making an Offer of Securities or a Reporting Entity as specified in the Direction.

Wakala

Means a contract of agency in which one party appoints another party to perform a certain task on its behalf (i.e. to invest amounts in Shari'a- compliant income generating assets), usually for payment a fee or commission.
