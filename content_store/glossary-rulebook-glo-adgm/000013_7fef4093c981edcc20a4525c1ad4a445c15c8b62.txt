Means a Sukuk that meets the requirements of MKT 4.15.3 when read together with MKT 4.16.

Means the designation provided by the Regulator to an ADGM Sustainability-Linked Sukuk pursuant to MKT 4.15.7 to 4.15.11 when read together with MKT 4.16.
