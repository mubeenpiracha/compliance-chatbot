Precise

Has the meaning given in section 95(5) of FSMR.

Preference Security

Securities which are given preference, in terms of dividend or distribution, over Ordinary Securities.

Premium Liability

Means the liability referred to in PIN 5.4.7.

Prescribed Market

Means any Recognised Investment Exchange, MTF or OTF.

Price Notation

Means the currency in which the price is expressed.

Price Reporting Agency

An agency or an index provider which constructs, compiles, assesses or reports, on a regular and systematic basis, prices of Investments, rates, indices, commodities, Accepted Virtual Assets, Accepted Spot Commodities or figures, which are made available to users.
