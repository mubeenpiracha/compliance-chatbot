ADGM Green Portfolio Rules

Means COBS 3.8.2 to 3.8.9

ADGM Green Sukuk

Means a Sukuk that meets the requirements of MKT 4.15.2 when read together with MKT 4.16.

ADGM Green Sukuk Designation

Means the designation provided by the Regulator to an ADGM Green Sukuk pursuant to MKT 4.15.7 to 4.15.11 when read together with MKT 4.16.

ADGM Incorporated Insurer

Means an Insurer that is incorporated in ADGM.

ADGM Sustainability- Linked Debenture

Means a Debenture that meets the requirements of MKT 4.15.3.
