Relevant Person

Has the meaning given in section 258 of FSMR.

Relevant Provisions

Means, in relation to COBS, "relevant provisions" means any provisions of the default rules of a Non-ADGM Recognised Clearing House which provide for the transfer of the positions or assets of a defaulting clearing member; are not necessary for the purposes of complying with the minimum requirements of Part 12 of FSMR; and may be relevant to a question falling to be determined in accordance with the laws of ADGM.

Relevant Requirements Means the requirements specified in section 163 and 164 of FSMR.
