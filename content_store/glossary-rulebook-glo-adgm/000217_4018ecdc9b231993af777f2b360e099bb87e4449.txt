Sponsor

In relation to:

(a) MKT, means a person approved by the Regulator to be a sponsor for the purposes of Rules made under section 83 of FSMR; and

(b) PRU, means an Authorised Person that repackages third party assets directly into a securitisation scheme. Where an Authorised Person repackages noninvestment Grade third party assets, it may fall within the definition of an Originator unless it originates or repackages no more than 10% of the scheme's total assets.

Spot Commodity

Has the meaning given in section 258 of FSMR.
