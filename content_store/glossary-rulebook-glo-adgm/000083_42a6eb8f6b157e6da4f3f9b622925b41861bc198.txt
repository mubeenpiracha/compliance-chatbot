Definitions

Means verification of customer identity by way of non-face-to-face, electronic means only.

Means the technology and associated processes used to undertake eKYC.

Means an undertaking or any other legal person that issues payment in the form of Electronic Money.

Means a Payment Transaction initiated by a Payer to pay for goods or services using electronic means without the simultaneous physical presence of the Payer and the Payee to the Payment Transaction.
