Investment-Linked Insurance

Means Contracts of Insurance where the benefits are wholly or partly to be determined by reference to the value of, or the income from, property of any description (whether or not specified in the contracts) or by reference to fluctuations in, or in an index of, the value of property of any description (whether or not so specified).

IOSCO

Means the International Organisation of Securities Commissions.

IOSCO Principles for Financial Market Infrastructures

Means a set of principles for Financial Markets Infrastructure issued by the International Organisation of Securities Commissions (IOSCO).
