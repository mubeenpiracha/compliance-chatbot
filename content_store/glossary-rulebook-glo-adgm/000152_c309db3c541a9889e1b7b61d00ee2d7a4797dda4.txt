Overallotment Facility

Means, for the purposes of MKT Chapter 6, a clause in the underwriting agreement or lead management agreement which permits acceptance of subscriptions or Offers to purchase a greater number of Relevant Securities than originally offered.

Overseas Competent Authority

Means a competent authority responsible for the authorisation or supervision of Recognised Clearing Houses in a country or territory other than ADGM.

Oversight Committee

Means a committee of at least three individuals each of whom meets the suitability criteria prescribed in FUNDS 13.3.3.
