Specific Risk

Means the risk that losses on an Authorised Person's net long or short position in an individual Security may arise from a negative or positive price movement of that Security relative to the relevant market generally.

Specified Benchmark

Means a Benchmark specified by the Regulator as a Specified Benchmark pursuant to COBS 21.2.1.

Specified Information

Has the meaning given in section 258 of FSMR.
