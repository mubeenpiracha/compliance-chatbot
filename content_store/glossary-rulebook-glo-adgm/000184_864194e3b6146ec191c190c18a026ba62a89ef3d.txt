Recognition Order

Has the meaning given in section 258 of FSMR.

Recognition Requirements

Means the requirements set out in MIR Chapters 2 to 4 in relation to the granting of a Recognition Order under section 120 of FSMR to a Recognised Body.

Reference Date

Means the date as at which an actuarial investigation is performed for the purposes of PIN 7.3.

Reference Entity

Means the entity against which payments under a Derivative Contract are calculated.

Reference Exchange Rate

Means the exchange rate that is used as the basis to calculate any currency exchange and that is made available by the Payment Service Provider or comes from a publicly available source.
