Calculation of E* for collateralised transactions under FCCA

A4.3.2 An Authorised Firm using the FCCA must, subject to A4.3.3, calculate the Exposure amount after risk mitigation, E*, of a collateralised transaction other than transactions referred to in Rule 4.14A1(a) to (c), using the following formula:

E∗ = max{0,[E ∙ (1 + He) − C ∙ (1 − Hc − Hfx]}

where:

E =

fair value of the Exposure, determined in accordance with section 4.9
