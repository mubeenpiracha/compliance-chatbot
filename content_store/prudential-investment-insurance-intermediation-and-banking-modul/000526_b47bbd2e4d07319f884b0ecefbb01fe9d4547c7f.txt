Application

This chapter applies to an Authorised Firm in Category 1, 2, 3A or 5 as follows:

(a)

sections 5.2 to 5.11 apply to an Authorised Firm in Category 1 or 2 (other than a Matched Principal);

(b)

sections 5.2 and 5.6 apply to an Authorised Firm in Category 2 (if it meets the definition of Matched Principal) or 3A; and

(c)

sections 5.2, 5.3 and 5.5 to 5.11 apply to an Authorised Firm in Category 5.
