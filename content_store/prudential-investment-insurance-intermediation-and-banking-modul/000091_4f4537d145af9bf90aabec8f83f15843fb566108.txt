(iv) Managing a PSIA (which is a PSIAr);

(v) Providing Trust Services (where it is acting as trustee in respect of at

(vi) Providing Money Services (where it issues Stored Value); and

(b)

it does not meet the criteria of Categories 1, 2, 3A, 3B or 5.

Guidance

A Category 3C Authorised Firm may be authorised to conduct other Financial Services, but it is the authorisation for Managing Assets, Managing a Collective Investment Fund, Providing Custody other than for a Fund or in relation to Crypto Assets, Managing a PSIA which is a PSIAr, Providing Trust Services (where it is acting as a trustee in respect of at least one express trust), or Providing Money Services (where it issues Stored Value) and the absence of authorisation for the activities specified in Rules 1.3.1, 1.3.2, 1.3.3 and 1.3.4 that are determinative of its belonging to Category 3C.
