Where the underlying pool of a securitisation comprises revolving and term Exposures, an Authorised Firm must apply the amortisation treatment outlined below for determining applicable regulatory capital only to that portion of the underlying pool containing revolving Exposures.

PRUDENTIAL – INVESTMENT, INSURANCE INTERMEDIATION AND BANKING (PIB)

4.14.53 An Authorised Firm which is the Originator of a Revolving Securitisation that includes economic triggers for Early Amortisation may regard the Exposures as transferred for the period up to the point of repayment, provided that:

(a)

during the amortisation period there is full sharing of interest, principal, expenses, losses and recoveries; and

(b)

the Authorised Firm’s risk management system provides warning indicators when economic or non-economic triggers may be activated.
