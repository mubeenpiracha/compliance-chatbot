2.

An Authorised Firm’s Operational Risk policy should, amongst other things, include consideration of Principles for the Sound Management of Operational Risk, issued by the Basel Committee on Banking Supervision (BCBS) and the Guidelines on the management of Operational Risk in market-related activities issued by the European Banking Authority which are useful in relation to activities other than banking.
