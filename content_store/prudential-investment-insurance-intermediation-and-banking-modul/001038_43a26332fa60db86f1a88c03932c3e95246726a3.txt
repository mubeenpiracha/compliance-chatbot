A5.3.32 For the purposes of Rule A5.3.31, a broad-based index means an index specified in

the table under (c) or an index that satisfies the following criteria:

(a)

the index contains at least 20 shares;

(b)

the weighting of the largest company is not greater than 20% of the total index; and

(c)

the weighting of the largest five companies is not greater than 60% of the total index.
