Application

This chapter applies to an Authorised Firm as follows:

(a)

sections 6.1 to 6.9 apply to an Authorised Firm in any Category;

(b)

sections 6.10 and 6.11 apply only to an Authorised Firm in Category 1, 2, 3A or 5;

(c)

section 6.12 applies only to an Authorised Firm in Category 3B, 3C, 3D or 4 which undertakes one or more of the following Financial Services:

(i)

Arranging Deals in Investments;

(ii) Managing Assets;

(iii) Advising on Financial Products;

(iv) Managing a Collective Investment Fund;
