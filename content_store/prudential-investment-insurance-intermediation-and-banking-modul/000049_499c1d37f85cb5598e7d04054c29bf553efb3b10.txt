CCyB Authority

(1) For the State, means the Central Bank; and (2) For any other jurisdiction, means the authority in that jurisdiction responsible for setting CCyB Rates.

CCyB Rate

A rate expressed as a percentage of Risk Weighted Assets that applies under Rules 3.9A.7 to 3.9A.9.

CCyB Requirement

The requirement to maintain a Countercyclical Capital Buffer under section 3.9A.

Counterparty

Means any person with or for whom an Authorised Firm carries on, or intends to carry on, any regulated business or associated business. In this context, Counterparty includes an individual, unincorporated body, company, government, local authority or other public body.
