Risk mitigation

56. Various methods can be used to mitigate Credit Risk, such as taking security or Collateral, obtaining a guarantee from a third party, purchasing insurance or Credit Derivatives. Authorised Firms should view these as complementary to, rather than a replacement for, thorough credit analysis and procedures.
