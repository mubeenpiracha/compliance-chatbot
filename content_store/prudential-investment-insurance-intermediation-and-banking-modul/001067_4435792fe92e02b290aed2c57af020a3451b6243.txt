2.

An Authorised Firm which trades in exotic options (e.g. barriers, digitals) would use either the scenario approach or the Internal Models Approach (IMA) to calculate its Market Risk Capital Requirement for such options, unless it is able to demonstrate to the DFSA that the Delta-plus method is appropriate. In the case of options on futures or forwards, the relevant underlying is that on which the future or forward is based (e.g. for a bought call option on a June 3-month bill future, the relevant underlying is the 3-month bill).
