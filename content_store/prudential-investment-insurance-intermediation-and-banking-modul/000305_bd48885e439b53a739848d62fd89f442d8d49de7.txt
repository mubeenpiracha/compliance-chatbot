Simplified Approach

Category 3A firms

4.7.1

(1)

This Rule applies only to an Authorised Firm in Category 3A.

(2)

Subject to (3) and (4), an Authorised Firm must apply the Simplified Approach as prescribed in section A4.12 in App4.

(3)

An Authorised Firm is not required to apply the Simplified Approach if it obtains prior approval of the DFSA not to do so.

(4)

After obtaining approval under (3), a firm must not revert to the Simplified Approach without further prior approval from the DFSA.
