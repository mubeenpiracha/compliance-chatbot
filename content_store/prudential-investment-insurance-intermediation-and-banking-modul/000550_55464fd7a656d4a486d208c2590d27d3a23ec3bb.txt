Risk management framework and governance

(1)

An Authorised Firm must implement and maintain an Operational Risk policy which enables it to identify, assess, control and monitor Operational Risk.

(2)

The policy must be documented and provide for a sound and well-defined risk management framework to address the Authorised Firm’s Operational Risk.

(3)

An Authorised Firm must:

(a) ensure that its risk management systems enable it to implement the

Operational Risk policy;
