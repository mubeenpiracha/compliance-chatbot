A4.3.4 For the purpose of Rule A4.3.3, a qualifying bilateral netting agreement is a netting

agreement with a Counterparty which:

(a) provides the non-defaulting party the right to terminate and close out in a timely manner all transactions under the agreement upon an event of default, including in the event of insolvency or bankruptcy of the counterparty;

(b) provide for the netting of gains and losses on transactions (including the value of any Collateral) terminated and closed out under it so that a single net amount is owed by one party to the other;

(c)

allow for the prompt liquidation or set-off of Collateral upon the event of default; and

(d)

is legally enforceable in each relevant jurisdiction upon the occurrence of an event of default and regardless of the counterparty’s insolvency or bankruptcy.
