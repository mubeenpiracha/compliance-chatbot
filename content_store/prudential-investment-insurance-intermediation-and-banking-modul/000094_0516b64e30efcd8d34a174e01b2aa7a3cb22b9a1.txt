Guidance

An Authorised Firm in Category 4 may not be authorised to conduct any other Financial Service beyond those listed in Rule 1.3.6(a); if it were so authorised it would belong to another Category.

Category 5

An Authorised Firm is in Category 5 if it:

(a)

is an Islamic Financial Institution; and

(b)

Manages a PSIAu.

Guidance

Authorised Firms in Categories 1 to 4 may also carry out Islamic Financial Business, but only those Authorised Firms in Categories 1 or 5 may Manage a PSIAu. They will not fall within Category 5 unless the whole of the business is conducted in accordance with Shariâ€™a and they Manage a PSIAu.
