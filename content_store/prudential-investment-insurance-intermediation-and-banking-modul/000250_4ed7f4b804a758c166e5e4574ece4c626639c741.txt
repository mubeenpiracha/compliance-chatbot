Qualifying T1 Capital included in consolidated AT1 Capital

3.16.7 An Authorised Firm must determine the amount of qualifying T1 Capital of a Subsidiary that is included in consolidated AT1 Capital by subtracting from the qualifying T1 Capital of that Subsidiary included in consolidated T1 Capital, the minority interests of that Subsidiary that are included in consolidated CET1 Capital.
