Restrictions on ownership or control

Guidance

By Rule 8.1.1(1), this section applies to an Authorised Firm in any Category.

Parents of Category 1 and 5 Authorised Firms

8.5.1

(1)

No entity other than one of the following may be the Parent of, or any of the Parents of, an Authorised Firm in Category 1 or 5:

(a) another Authorised Firm in Category 1 or 5; or

(b) a Regulated Financial Institution licensed to carry on the activities of

accepting deposits.

(2)

An entity other than one referred to in (1)(a) or (b) may be the Parent of an Authorised Firm in Category 1 or 5 where the ultimate, or any intermediate, Parent is an entity of the type specified under 1(a) or (b).
