Subordinated debt, equity and other capital instruments

4.12.18 (1) An Authorised Firm must categorise as an equity Exposure:

(a) any instrument constituting an investment in subordinated debt, equity or

other capital instrument if:

(i)

the return of invested funds can be achieved only by the sale of the investment or sale of the rights to the investment or by the liquidation of the issuer;

(ii)

it does not place an obligation on the issuer; and

(iii)

it conveys a residual claim on the assets or income of the issuer;

(b) any instrument with the same structure as those permitted as T1 Capital

if issued by an Authorised Firm;

(c)

any instrument that places an obligation on the issuer and meets any of the following conditions:

(i)

the issuer may defer indefinitely the settlement of the obligation;
