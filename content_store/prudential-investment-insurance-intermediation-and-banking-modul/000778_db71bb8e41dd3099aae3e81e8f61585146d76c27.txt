PRUDENTIAL – INVESTMENT, INSURANCE INTERMEDIATION AND BANKING (PIB)

A2.5 Prudent Valuation Practices

Guidance

1.

This following guidance sets out the DFSA’s expectations for prudent valuation practices, including adequate systems and controls and valuation methodologies, for the purpose of section 2.4 of PIB.

Systems and controls

2.

Authorised Firms should establish and maintain adequate systems and controls sufficient to give management and the DFSA the confidence that their valuation estimates are prudent and reliable.
