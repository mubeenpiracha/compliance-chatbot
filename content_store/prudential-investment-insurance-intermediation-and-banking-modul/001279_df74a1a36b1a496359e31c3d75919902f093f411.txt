A10.3 Supervisory Review and Evaluation Process (SREP)

Guidance

Application

1.

This Guidance is relevant to an Authorised Firm to whom section 10.5 applies (that is, a firm in Category 1, 2 or 5) in regard to a Supervisory Review and Evaluation Process (referred to in this Guidance as a SREP).

Introduction

2.

This Guidance covers the evaluation criteria and methodology (referred to as a SREP) that the DFSA may use when reviewing and evaluating the IRAP and ICAAP of an Authorised Firm.
