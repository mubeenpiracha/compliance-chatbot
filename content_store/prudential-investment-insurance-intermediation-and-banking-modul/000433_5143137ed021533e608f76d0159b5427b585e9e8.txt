(2)

Where the credit event under (1)(f) does not include the restructuring of the underlying obligation under (1)(f)(iii), an Authorised Firm may partly recognise the effects of Credit Risk mitigation of any Credit Derivative as follows:

(a)

if the amount that the protection provider has undertaken to pay is lower than or equal to the amount of the Exposure, the Authorised Firm may recognise 60% of the value of the Credit Risk mitigation; or

(b)

if the amount that the protection provider has undertaken to pay is higher than the amount of the Exposure, the Authorised Firm may recognise the Credit Risk mitigation up to 60% of the Exposure value.
