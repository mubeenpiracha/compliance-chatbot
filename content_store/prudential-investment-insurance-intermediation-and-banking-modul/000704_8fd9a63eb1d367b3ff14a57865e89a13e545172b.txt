Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR) Requirement

(1)

An Authorised Firm must maintain a Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR) of at least 100%.

(2)

The NSFR under (1) must be calculated using the formula:

NSFR = ASF x 100

RSF

where:

(a) ASF (Available Stable Funding) is the amount, calculated in accordance with Rule A9.4.1, representing the relative stability of an Authorised Firm’s available funding sources; and

(b) RSF (Required Stable Funding) is the amount, calculated in accordance with Rule A9.4.2, representing the Liquidity Risk profile of an Authorised Firm’s assets and OBS Exposures (or potential liquidity Exposures).
