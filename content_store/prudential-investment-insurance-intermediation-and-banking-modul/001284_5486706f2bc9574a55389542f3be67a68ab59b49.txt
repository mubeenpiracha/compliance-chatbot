Evaluation of the IRAP and ICAAP

10.

The SREP evaluation of the IRAP and, where applicable, the ICAAP covers all activities of an Authorised Firm and takes all relevant data collected during the supervisory process into account. The SREP evaluation process will use desk based reviews, firm visits and meetings to arrive at a final view.
