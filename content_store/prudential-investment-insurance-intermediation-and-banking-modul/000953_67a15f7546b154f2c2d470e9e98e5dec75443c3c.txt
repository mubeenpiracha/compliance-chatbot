Examples of a Counterparty include:

a.

the customer or borrower;

b.

where the Authorised Firm is providing a guarantee, the person guaranteed;

c.

for a Derivatives contract, the person with whom the contract was made;

d.

for most exchange-traded contracts involving a central clearing mechanism, that central clearing mechanism; and

e.

where a bill held by an Authorised Firm has been accepted by another Financial Institution, the acceptor.
