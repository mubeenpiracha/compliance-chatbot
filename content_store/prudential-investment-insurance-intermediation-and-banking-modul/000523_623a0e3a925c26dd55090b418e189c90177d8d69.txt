MARKET RISK

Introduction

Guidance

1.

This chapter addresses the regulatory requirements in respect of managing the Market Risk exposures of an Authorised Firm. Market Risk refers to the risk of incurring losses on positions due to adverse changes in market prices or in underlying value drivers. This chapter aims to ensure that an Authorised Firm engaging in activities exposing the firm to risks associated with potential adverse movements in market prices adopts appropriate and effective risk management practices and holds regulatory capital of the right quality that is also commensurate with the risks involved.
