Close Links

A Person (Person A) has Close Links with a Person (Person B) if:

(a) Person B:

(i)

is a Holding Company of Person A;

(ii) is a Subsidiary of Person A;

(iii) is a Holding Company of the Subsidiary of Person

A;

(iv) is a Subsidiary of a Holding Company of Person

A; or

(v) owns and controls 20% or more of the voting

rights or shares of Person A; or

(b) Person A owns and controls 20% or more of the voting

rights or shares of Person B.

Closely Related

Has the meaning given in Rule A4.11.5.
