Operational capability to monetise assets referred to in Rule A9.2.3(b), requires procedures and appropriate systems to be in place. This includes providing the liquidity management function with access to all necessary information to monetise any asset at any time. Monetisation of the asset should be executable operationally within the standard settlement period for the asset class in the relevant jurisdiction.
