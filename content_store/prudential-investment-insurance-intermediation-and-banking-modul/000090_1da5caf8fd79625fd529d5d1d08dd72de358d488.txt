Guidance

A Category 3B Authorised Firm may be authorised to conduct other Financial Services, but it is the authorisation for Providing Custody for a Fund, Providing Custody of Crypto Assets, Acting as Trustee of a Fund or Operating or Acting as the Administrator of an Employee Money Purchase Scheme, and the absence of authorisation for the activities specified in Rules 1.3.1, 1.3.2 and 1.3.3 that are determinative of its belonging to Category 3B.
