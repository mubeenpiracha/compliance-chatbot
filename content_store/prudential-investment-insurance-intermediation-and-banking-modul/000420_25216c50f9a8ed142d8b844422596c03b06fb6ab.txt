Guidance

In determining if there is a material positive correlation as referred to in Rule 4.13.8(c), an Authorised Firm should take into account several factors, including the consistency of any correlation over time, the incidental nature of any correlation (whether it will hold for over a one-year horizon) or whether any correlation may lead to larger or smaller swings in potential credit losses.
