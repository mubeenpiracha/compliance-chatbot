Disclosure of Inside Information

Immediate Disclosure of Inside Information

7.2.1

(1)

Subject to Rule 7.2.1(4), once a Reporting Entity is, or becomes, aware of Inside Information, it must immediately Disclose that Inside Information.

(2)

A Reporting Entity must ensure that the Disclosure it makes pursuant to (1) is not false, misleading, or deceptive and does not omit anything likely to affect the import of the Inside Information.
