Shares in public hands

2.3.10 (1)

If an application is made for the admission of a class of Shares, a sufficient number of Shares of that class must, no later than the time of admission, be in public hands.

(2)

For the purposes of (1), a sufficient number of Shares will be taken to have been distributed to the public according to the following thresholds:

(a)

In the case of a Market Capitalisation of the Issuer of under $500 million, when 20% of the Shares for which application for admission has been made are in public hands;

(b)

In the case of a Market Capitalisation of the Issuer of $500 million or more and under $1 billion, when 15% of the Shares for which application for admission has been made are in public hands; and

(c)

In the case of a Market Capitalisation of the Issuer of $1 billion or more, when 12% of the Shares for which application for admission has been made are in public hands.
