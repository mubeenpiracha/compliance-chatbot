4.14 Retail Debentures or Sukuk

4.14.1 A Person may only make a Prospectus Offer of a Retail Debenture or Sukuk where:

(1)

the Issuer of the Retail Debenture or Sukuk has applied for its admission to trading on a Recognised Investment Exchange; and

(2)

the Issuer or guarantor of the Retail Debenture or Sukuk has received an investment grade credit rating from an authorised Credit Rating Agency or such other credit rating agency considered acceptable by the Regulator.

Guidance

The Regulator may consider a credit rating agency to be acceptable if it is regulated in a Zone 1 jurisdiction.
