Quarterly cash reports

10.1.3B (1)

A Reporting Entity must complete MKT Form 10-1 on a quarterly basis if the Regulator:

(a)

requires it as a condition of admission to the Official List pursuant to section 52(1)(b) of FSMR;

(b)

classifies the Reporting Entity as a Mining Exploration Reporting Entity or a Petroleum Exploration Reporting Entity; or

(c)

requires it pursuant to section 84 of FSMR.
