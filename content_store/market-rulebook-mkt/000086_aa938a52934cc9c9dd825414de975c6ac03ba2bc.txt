Shares in public hands

2.7.2

(1)

A Listed Entity must ensure that a sufficient number of its Shares are distributed to the public at all times.

(2)

A Listed Entity which no longer complies with (1) must notify the Regulator as soon as possible after it first becomes aware of its non-compliance.

Guidance

Rules 2.3.10(2) and (3) describe the circumstances which a firm must meet for a sufficient number of its Shares to be distributed to the public.
