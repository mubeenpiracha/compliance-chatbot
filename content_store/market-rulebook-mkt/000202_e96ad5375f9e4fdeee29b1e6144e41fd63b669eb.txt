Termination of appointment

5.1.11 Where a Person who is required to appoint a Sponsor dismisses the Sponsor, the Person must advise the Regulator in writing without delay of the dismissal, giving details of any relevant facts and circumstances.

5.1.12 Where a Sponsor resigns, it must advise the Regulator in writing without delay of the

resignation, giving details of any relevant facts and circumstances.
