Content and procedure of the Disclosure

3.6.3 A Disclosure made by a Connected Person must contain the:

(1)

name and address of the Connected Person;

(2)

name and address of the Reporting Entity and its registered address;

(3)

name and registered address of the Listed Fund;

(4)

date on which the event giving rise to the obligation to make a Disclosure occurred;

(6)

3.6.4 Upon a Connected Person making a Disclosure to the Reporting Entity, the Reporting Entity must, as soon as possible, make a Disclosure of that information in accordance with Rule 7.7.1.
