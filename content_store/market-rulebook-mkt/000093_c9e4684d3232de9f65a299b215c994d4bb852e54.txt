In (2), "Treasury Shares" means Shares which are:

(a)

admitted to the Official List;

(b)

held by the same Company which issued the Shares; and

(c)

purchased by the Company in (b) using its distributable profits.

Other on-going requirements

2.7.7 A Listed Entity must ensure that at all times:

(1)

its business remains suitable for admission to the Official List;

(2)

subject to Rule 2.3.6(2), it can operate its business independently of a controlling Shareholder and any Associate; and

(3)

it has adequate systems and controls to eliminate or manage material conflicts of interest in its business on an on-going basis.
