Guidance

1. Prior to the appointment of an external review provider under Rule 4.15.6 an Issuer should take reasonable steps to ensure that the external review provider:

a. has the required skills, resources and experience to undertake the review; and

b. is independent of, and not subject to, any conflict of interest with respect to the

Issuer.

2. When arranging for an external review under Rule 4.15.6, an Issuer should take into consideration the ICMA Guidelines for Green, Social and Sustainability Debentures Reviews.
