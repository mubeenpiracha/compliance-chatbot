Guidance

The Regulator would consider that a document has been made available to the public if, following its Disclosure, the document is available on the Listed Entity's website or on the website of the Recognised Investment Exchange on which its Securities are admitted to trading.

Contact details

2.8.6 A Reporting Listed Entity must ensure that the Regulator is provided with up to date contact details of appropriate persons nominated by it to act as the first point of contact with the Regulator in relation to the Listed Entity's compliance with the Rules and FSMR, as applicable.
