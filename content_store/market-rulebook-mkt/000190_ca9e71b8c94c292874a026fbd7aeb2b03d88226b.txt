Guidance

1. Once the Regulator has made a decision in relation to an application, it will without undue delay inform the Issuer in writing of its decision.

2. The Regulator may refuse to grant an application for an ADGM Green Bond Designation or an ADGM Sustainability-Linked Bond Designation if it is not satisfied that the requirements of this section have been met or will be met on an ongoing basis. Annual submission

4.15.12In order to retain the ADGM Green Bond Designation or ADGM Sustainability-Linked Bond Designation, an Issuer must submit a copy of the post-issuance external review conducted under Rule 4.15.6(2) to the Regulator as soon as reasonably practicable following completion of the review.
