(5)

An issue of Securities under a Security Purchase Plan. This is available only once in any 12 month period, and where:

(a)

the number of Securities to be issued is not greater than 20% of the number of fully paid Ordinary Securities on issue;

(b)

Offers are made to holders of Ordinary Securities in an amount not exceeding $10,000 in value per holder; and

(c)

the issue price of the Securities is at least 80% of the volume weighted average market price for Securities in that class calculated over the last five days on which sales in the Securities were recorded, either before the day on which the issue was Disclosed or before the day on which the issue was made.
