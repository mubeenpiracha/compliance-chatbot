4.15.9 If, at any time between the filing of an application and the grant of an ADGM Green Bond Designation or ADGM Sustainability-Linked Bond Designation, the Issuer becomes aware of any material change, error, or omission reasonably likely to be relevant to the application under consideration, it must inform the Regulator in writing of such change without delay.

(1)

make any enquiries which independent of the Issuer; or

including enquiries

(2)

require the Issuer to provide further information.

4.15.11Upon grant of an ADGM Green Bond Designation or ADGM Sustainability-Linked Bond Designation, the Regulator will publish the name and details of the Security on a register maintained on its website.
