Development and monitoring of the Remuneration structure

1.

To ensure that the Remuneration structure and strategies of the Authorised Person or Recognised Body are appropriate to the nature, scale and complexity of the Authorised Person or Recognised Body's business, the Governing Body should take account of the risks to which the Authorised Person or Recognised Body could be exposed as a result of the conduct or behaviour of its Employees. The Governing Body should play an active role in developing the Remuneration strategy and policies of the Authorised Person or Recognised Body. A Remuneration committee of the Governing Body could play an important role in the development of the Authorised Person or Recognised Body's Remuneration structure and strategy.
