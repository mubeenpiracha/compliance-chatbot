(3)

The Regulator may require the Authorised Person to obtain written confirmation from its Auditor that the change of financial year end would not result in any significant distortion of the financial position of the Authorised Person.

If an Authorised Person is not a Domestic Firm and intends to change its financial year, it must provide the Regulator with reasonable advance notice prior to the change taking effect.
