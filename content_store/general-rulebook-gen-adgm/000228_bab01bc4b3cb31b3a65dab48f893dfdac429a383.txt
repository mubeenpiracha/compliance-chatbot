Requirement for prior approval of Controllers of Domestic Firms

(1)

In the case of an Authorised Person which is a Domestic Firm, a Person must not:

(a)

become a Controller; or

(b)

increase the level of control which that Person has in the Domestic Firm beyond a threshold specified in (2),

unless that Person has obtained the prior written approval of the Regulator to do so.
