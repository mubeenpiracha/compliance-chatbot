Where a Definitive Valuation produces a higher valuation of the net asset value of an In-Scope Entity than the Provisional Valuation, the Regulator may—

(a)

instruct a purchaser to pay additional consideration for any rights, assets, liabilities or Shares transferred under the Sale of Business Tool; or

(b)

modify any liability of—

(i)

in the case of an In-Scope Entity that is not an ADGM Branch, the In-Scope Entity; and

(ii)

in the case of an ADGM Branch, the Non-ADGM Institution,

which has been reduced, deferred or cancelled pursuant to the Write Down or Conversion Power or a Resolution Order so as to increase or reinstate that liability.
