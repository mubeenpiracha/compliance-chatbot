The exercise of the powers set out in subsections (n) and (q)(ii) shall be without prejudice to—

(a)

the right of an employee of the Institution in Resolution to terminate a contract of employment; or

(b)

subject to subsection (1) and sections 61 and 63(1), any right of a party to a contract to exercise rights under the contract, including the right to terminate, where entitled to do so in accordance with the terms of the contract by virtue of an act or omission by the Institution in Resolution or circumstances occurring, including prior to the relevant transfer, or by or with respect to the Recipient after the relevant transfer.
