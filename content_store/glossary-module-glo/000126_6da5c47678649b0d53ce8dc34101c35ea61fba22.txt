GLOSSARY (GLO)

Definition

(1)

In relation to an Offer of Securities to the Public, a document containing the information specified in Article 15 of the Markets Law 2012 and the MKT Rules, and includes a Supplementary Prospectus.

(2)

In relation to a Fund, a document containing the information prescribed under the Collective the CIR Rules, Investment Law 2010 and including or Supplementary Prospectus.
