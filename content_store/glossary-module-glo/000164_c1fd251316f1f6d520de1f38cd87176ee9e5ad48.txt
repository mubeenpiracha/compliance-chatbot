GLOSSARY (GLO)

Definition

Has the meaning given in GEN Rule A2.2.1(c).

Means all material (whether in the form of data stored on film, electronic media or other media or paper, otherwise) prepared by or for, or obtained by a Registered Auditor in connection with, the performance of the audit concerned and includes:

(a)

the record of audit procedures performed;

(b)

relevant audit evidence obtained; and

(c)

conclusions reached.
