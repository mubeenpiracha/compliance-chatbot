Nominee Company

A company incorporated in the DIFC whose business consists solely of acting as a holder of Client Assets where such assets are held by the Nominee Company as agent of an Authorised Firm.

Non-Cellular Assets

Assets of a Protected Cell Company which are not Cellular Assets.

Non-Cellular Liabilities

Liabilities that may not be settled by disposition of cellular assets.

Non-DIFC insurer

Non-DIFC Scheme

A Person, other than an Insurer, who is authorised and supervised as an insurer by a Financial Services Regulator. Means an Employee Money Purchase Scheme or any similar scheme or arrangement that is established outside the DIFC.
