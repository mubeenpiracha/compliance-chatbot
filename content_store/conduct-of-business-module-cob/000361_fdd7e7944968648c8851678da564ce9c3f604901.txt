9.3 Member access criteria

9.3.1

(1)

An ATS Operator may, subject to (2), (3) and (4), accept as a member:

(a)

an Authorised Firm;

(b)

a Recognised Member;

(c)

a Person who meets the criteria in GEN Rule 2.3.2(2);

(d)

a Person who is classified as a Professional Client pursuant to COB Rule 2.3.4(1)(g), (h) and (i); or

(e)

a Person not referred to in (a) to (d), only if:
