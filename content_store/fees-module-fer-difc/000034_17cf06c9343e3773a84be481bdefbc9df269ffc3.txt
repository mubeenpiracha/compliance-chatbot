2.10 Application relating to a business transfer scheme

2.10.1

(1) An Authorised Firm applying under GEN Rule 12.1.5 for DFSA consent to a transfer scheme must pay to the DFSA an application fee of $40,000.

(2) A Person applying for an order sanctioning a transfer scheme under Article 108 of the Regulatory Law must pay to the DFSA a fee of $30,000 on or before the date of seeking from the DFSA the nomination or approval of a person under Article 111(2) of that law.
