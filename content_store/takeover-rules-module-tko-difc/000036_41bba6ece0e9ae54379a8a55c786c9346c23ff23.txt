2.3.3 An announcement of a possible Bid must:

(a)

name the potential Bidder; and

(b)

state that the shareholders concerned should exercise caution in dealing in their Shares.

Guidance

The announcement of a possible Bid may be brief and couched in general terms and should, in the normal course, state that talks are taking place or that a potential Bidder is considering making a Bid or that an announcement is pending which could have a material effect on the price of the Shares.
