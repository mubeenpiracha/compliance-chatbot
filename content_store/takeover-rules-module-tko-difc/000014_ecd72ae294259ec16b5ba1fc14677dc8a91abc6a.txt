TAKEOVER RULES (TKO)

1.2 Application of these Rules

1.2.1 The Rules in this Module (TKO) apply to Persons to whom the Markets Law 2012

applies, and in particular:

(a)

Reporting Entities as Targets or potential Targets under the Rules;

(b)

Bidders or potential Bidders and any Person acting in concert with them;

(c)

Directors and officers of Targets and Bidders; and

(d)

professional advisers in relation to a Takeover transaction.
