Guidance

The DFSA may waive or modify the application of Rules 4.2.1 or 4.2.2 if it is satisfied that circumstances are appropriate. See Chapter 16 of these Rules.

4.3 Consideration to be offered

4.3.1 Mandatory Bids made under these Rules must, in respect of each class of Shares involved, be in cash or be accompanied by a cash alternative at not less than the highest price paid by the Bidder or any Person acting in concert with it for Shares of that class during the Bid Period and within the preceding six months.
