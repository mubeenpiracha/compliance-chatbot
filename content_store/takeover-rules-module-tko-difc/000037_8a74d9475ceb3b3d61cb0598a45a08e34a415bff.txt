Guidance

The respective obligations of the Target and of the Bidder to make announcements under these Rules may be satisfied by the making of a joint announcement.

2.4.2 A Target must make an announcement when the Governing Body of the Target has been notified of a firm intention to make a Bid from a serious source, irrespective of the attitude of the Governing Body to the Bid.
