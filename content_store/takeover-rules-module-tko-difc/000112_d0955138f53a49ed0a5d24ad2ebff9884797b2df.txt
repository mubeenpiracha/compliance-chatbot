12.1.2 The restrictions in Rule 12.1.1 also apply following a partial Bid which could result in a holding of not less than the 30% and not more than 50% of the voting rights of the Target whether or not the Bid has become or been declared unconditional. When such a Bid has become or been declared unconditional, the period of 12 months runs from that date.
