Guidance

The general responsibilities of an Audit Principal are defined in Article 97(c) of the Regulatory Law.

Application for registration

2.4.1 When applying for registration as an Audit Principal, both the relevant individual and the Registered Auditor must complete and submit the appropriate form on the DFSA electronic portal, supported by such additional materials as may be required by the DFSA, to the DFSA.
