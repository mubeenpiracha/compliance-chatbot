Auditor Module (AUD)

2A

APPOINTMENT OF A SENIOR OFFICER

2A.1 Application

2A.1.1 This chapter applies to every Registered Auditor.

2A.2 Appointment of a Senior Officer

2A.2.1

(1) A Registered Auditor must ensure that at all times a person is appointed as the senior

officer (“Senior Officer”) of the Registered Auditor.

(2) The Registered Auditor must ensure that the Senior Officer appointed under (1):

(i)

is a person with responsibility for the day-to-day management, supervision and control of the Registered Auditor’s activities carried on in or from the DIFC;
