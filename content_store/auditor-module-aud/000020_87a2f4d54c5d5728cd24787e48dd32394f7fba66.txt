Guidance

If a Registered Auditor breaches a Principle, the DFSA may consider taking appropriate action on that basis, including suspending or withdrawing the registration of the Registered Auditor.

Principle 1 – Integrity

2.3A.2 A Registered Auditor must observe high standards of integrity and fair dealing.

Principle 2 – Due skill, care and diligence

2.3A.3

In conducting its business activities a Registered Auditor must act with due skill, care and diligence and in accordance with applicable technical and professional standards.
