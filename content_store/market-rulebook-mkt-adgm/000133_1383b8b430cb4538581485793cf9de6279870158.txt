The Product Summary Note must:

(a)

highlight key information in the Offer document to investors;

(b)

clearly disclose required information in the format as set out in APP 7;

(c)

give a fair and balanced view of the nature, material benefits and material risks of the Securities offered;

(d)

not contain any statement or information that is false or misleading in the form and context in which it is included;

(e)

be given together with the Offer document to investors. If the Issuer makes the Offer document available online, the Product Summary Note must be made available online together with the Offer document;

(f)

not contain any information that is not contained in the Offer document;

(g)

not contain any Inside Information that differs in any material particular from that set out in the Offer document; and

(h)

not omit any information from any part of the Product Summary Note which would result in that part of the Product Summary Note being construed as false or misleading.

(2)

The Product Summary Note shall contain the prominent disclaimer set out in Rule 4.3.4 in bold, on its front.
