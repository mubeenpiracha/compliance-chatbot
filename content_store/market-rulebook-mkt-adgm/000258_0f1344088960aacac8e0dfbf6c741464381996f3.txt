(3)

In the case of a Person who is a Connected Person under Rule 7.3.2(1)(b), that Person must make the Connected Person Disclosure upon:

(a)

acquiring or ceasing to hold voting Securities carrying more than 5% of the voting rights attaching to all voting Securities of either the Reporting Entity or a Controller of the Reporting Entity; and

(b)

an increase or decrease of at least 1% of the level of interest previously reported pursuant to (a).

7.3.4 For the purposes of Rules 7.3.2 and 7.3.3, a Person is taken to hold Financial Instruments in or relating to a Reporting Entity, if the Person holds a Financial Instrument that on its maturity will confer on him:

(1)

an unconditional right to acquire the Financial Instrument; or

(2)

the discretion as to his right to acquire the Financial Instrument.
