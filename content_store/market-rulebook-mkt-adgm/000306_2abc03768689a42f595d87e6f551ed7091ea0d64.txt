Restricted Securities

Application

9.6.1 This section applies, subject to Rule 9.6.2, to:

(1)

an Applicant for admission of Securities to the Official List;

(2)

a relevant Listed Entity; and

(3)

any Person that holds, or will hold, Securities that are Restricted Securities in accordance with APP 8 or which the Regulator has deemed to be Restricted Securities by giving notice to the relevant Applicant or Listed Entity.
