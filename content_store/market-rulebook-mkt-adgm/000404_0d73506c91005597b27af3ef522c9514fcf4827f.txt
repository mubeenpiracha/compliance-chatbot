12.3 Prospectus – Petroleum disclosures

12.3.1 In addition to complying with Chapter 4, a Prospectus must:

(1)

if it includes a statement about Petroleum Resources that includes estimates of Petroleum Reserves, Contingent Resources or Prospective Resources, comply with Rule 12.2; and

(2)

include details in relation to the Petroleum Reporting Entity’s policies and practices in relation to operating in a sustainable manner, including:

(i)

the Petroleum Reporting Entity’s policy with regards to environmental and social issues;
