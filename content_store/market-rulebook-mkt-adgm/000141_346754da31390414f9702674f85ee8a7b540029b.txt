4.5

Prospectus structure and content

Guidance

1.

Where the term "Prospectus Offer" is used in this section in reference to a Person, such a Person is either making an Offer of Securities or seeking to have Securities admitted to trading on a Recognised Investment Exchange.

2.

The Regulator will only permit a Prospectus Offer of a Retail Debenture or Sukuk in the circumstance described in Rule 4.14.
