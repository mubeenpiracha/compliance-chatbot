Guidance

The Regulator may withdraw an ADGM Green Bond Designation or ADGM Sustainability-Linked Bond Designation upon notification in accordance with Rule 4.15.14, pursuant to Rule 4.15.13.

4.16 ADGM Green Sukuk and ADGM Sustainability-Linked Sukuk

Application

4.16.1 The Rules and Guidance set out under section 4.15 will apply to Sukuk in the same way as to Debentures, with all references to ‘Debenture’ in section 4.15 being read as ‘Sukuk’, and the references in Rules 4.15.1 to 4.15.3 to paragraph 88 or 90 of Schedule 1 of FSMR being read as a reference to paragraph 89 of Schedule 1 of FSMR.

4.16.2 An ADGM Green Sukuk or ADGM Sustainability-Linked Sukuk may apply for, and maintain, an ADGM Green Sukuk Designation or ADGM Sustainability-Linked Sukuk Designation in the same way as for Debentures, with all references to ‘Debenture’ or ‘Bond’ in Rules 4.15.7 to 4.15.14 being read as ‘Sukuk’.
