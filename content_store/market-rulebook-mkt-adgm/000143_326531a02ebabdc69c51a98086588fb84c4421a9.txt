For the purposes of section 62 of FSMR, the Prospectus must:

(a)

present information in a form which is comprehensible and easy to analyse;

(b)

contain the documents and information specified in (1)(a) or (b) as are applicable; and

(c)

in the case of an Offer of Securities, have an application form that meets the requirement in Rule 4.5.5.
