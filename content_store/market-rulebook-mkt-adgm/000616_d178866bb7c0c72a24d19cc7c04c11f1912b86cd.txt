Remuneration committee

69.

The Board should establish and maintain a remuneration committee to assess the remuneration of Directors (including the chairman). The remuneration committee should comprise at least three members, with a majority of those members being independent nonexecutive Directors. The chair of the committee should be an independent nonexecutive Director. In addition, the chairman of the Board may also be a member but not the chair of the committee.
