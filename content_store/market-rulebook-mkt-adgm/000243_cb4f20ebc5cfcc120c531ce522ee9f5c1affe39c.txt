6.2.17 Price Stabilisation and Dual-listings

(1)

This Rule applies to a Person who carries out Price Stabilisation of dual-listed Relevant Securities.

(2)

For the purposes of (1), ‘dual-listed Relevant Securities’ are Relevant Securities which are listed concurrently on a Recognised Investment Exchange and on either a Remote Investment Exchange or an exchange in a jurisdiction other than ADGM.

Guidance

‘Dual-listed Relevant Securities’ in (2) would, in relation to one Listed Security, include Certificates (e.g., global depository receipts) and Warrants over the other Listed Security.
