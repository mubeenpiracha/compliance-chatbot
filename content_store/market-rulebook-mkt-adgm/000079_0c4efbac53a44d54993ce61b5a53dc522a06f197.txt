2.6.1 A Listed Entity which has had the admission to the Official List of any of its Securities suspended must continue to comply with all relevant Listing Rules applicable to it.

2.6.2

If the Regulator suspends the admission to the Official List of any Securities, it may impose such requirements on the procedure for lifting the suspension as it considers appropriate.
