Publication of a Prospectus

3.3.4 A Fund Prospectus approved by the Regulator pursuant to Rule 3.3.1 must:

(1)

be filed with the Recognised Investment Exchange on which the Units are to be admitted to trading as soon as possible after the Regulator has granted its approval; and

(2)

be published in accordance with the requirements in Rule 3.10.1.
