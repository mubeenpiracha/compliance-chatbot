Decision on Application

The Regulator may—

(a)

refuse an Application for Listing; or

(b)

impose conditions or restrictions, in respect of the admission of Securities to the Official List, or vary or withdraw such conditions or restrictions;

in the circumstances specified in subsection (2).

(a)

the Regulator reasonably considers, for a reason relating to the Issuer of the Securities or to the Securities, that—
