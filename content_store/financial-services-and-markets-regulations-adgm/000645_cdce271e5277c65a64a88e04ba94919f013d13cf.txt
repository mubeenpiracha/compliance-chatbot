Acting as the Trustee of an Investment Trust

(1)

Acting as the Trustee of an Investment Trust is a specified kind of activity.

(2)

A person Acts as the Trustee of an Investment Trust when the person holds the assets of a Collective Investment Fund on trust for the Unitholders where the Collective Investment Fund is in the form of an Investment Trust.
