Qualifying Property Transfers

means the property transfers to which these Regulations apply by virtue of section 152(1).

Rating Subject

has the meaning given to that term in paragraph 65(3) of Schedule 1.

Real Property

has the meaning given to that term in the Real Property Regulations 2015.

Recipient

is, for the purposes of Schedule 2, the person to whom the communication is made or, in the case of a non-real time communication which is directed at persons generally, any person who reads or hears the communication.
