63.

Regulator power to publish information

Where a person making a Prospectus Offer fails to publish any information which that person is required to publish by or under these Regulations, the Regulator may publish such information in the manner prescribed in the Rules.

64.

Use of foreign documents for purposes of making a Prospectus Offer32

No person shall use any document produced in accordance with the law applicable in another jurisdiction for the purposes of making a Prospectus Offer except in the circumstances prescribed in the Rules.
