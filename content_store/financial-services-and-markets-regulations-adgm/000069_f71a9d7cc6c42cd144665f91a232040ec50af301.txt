The Regulator may, on the application of an Authorised Person with a Financial Services Permission—

(a)

impose a new Requirement;

(b)

vary a Requirement imposed by the Regulator under this section; or

(c)

cancel such a Requirement.

The Regulator may refuse an application under subsection (5) if it appears to it that it is desirable to do so in order to further one or more of its objectives.

Requirements under section 35: further provisions

A Requirement may, in particular, be imposed—

(a)

so as to require the person concerned to take action specified by the Regulator; or

(b)

so as to require the person concerned to refrain from taking action specified by the Regulator.

A Requirement may extend to activities which are not Regulated Activities.
