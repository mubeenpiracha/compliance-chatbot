Relevant Authorised Person287

means, for the purposes of paragraph 22 of Schedule 1, an Authorised Person who has permission to Effect Contracts of Insurance or to Sell investments of the kind specified by paragraph 98 of Schedule 1, so far as relevant to such contracts.

Relevant Benchmark

means, for the purposes of section 104, a benchmark of a kind specified in the Rules made by the Board.

Relevant Complaint

means a complaint which the Regulator considers is relevant to the question of whether the body concerned should remain a Recognised Body.
