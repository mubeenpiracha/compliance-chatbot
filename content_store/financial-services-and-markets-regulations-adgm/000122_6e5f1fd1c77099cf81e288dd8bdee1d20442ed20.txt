Chapter 2 Obligations of Reporting Entities

72.

Definition of a Reporting Entity34

(1)

A person is, subject to subsection (3), a Reporting Entity if the person—

(a)

has or had Securities admitted to the Official List at any time;

(b)

has made an Offer of Securities other than in relation to Units of a Fund;

(c)

merges with or acquires a Reporting Entity referred to in paragraphs (a) or (b); or

(d)

is declared by the Regulator pursuant to subsection (4) to be a Reporting Entity.
