Financial Promotion Restriction

Financial Services Permission

is a permission given, or having effect as if so given, by the Regulator in accordance with Part 4.

FinTech228

means innovative financial technology.

FinTech Participant229

means an Authorised Person who has a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Developing Financial Technology Services within the RegLab.

FinTech Proposal230

means the financial technology innovation proposed to be developed and tested by the FinTech Participant.
