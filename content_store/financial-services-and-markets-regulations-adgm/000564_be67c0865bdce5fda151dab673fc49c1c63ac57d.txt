Relevant Transaction

means, for the purposes of paragraph 22 of Schedule 1, the effecting of a Contract of Insurance or the sale of an investment of the kind specified by paragraph 98 of Schedule 1, so far as relevant to such contracts.

Remote Body291

means a Remote Clearing House or Remote Investment Exchange which has been granted a Recognition Order by the Regulator in accordance with section 127.

Remote Body Applicant292

means, for the purposes of Part 12, a Body Corporate or association which has neither its head office nor its registered office in the Abu Dhabi Global Market and which has applied for a Recognition Order as a Remote Body.
