174. Power to apply provisions to other cases

(1)

A power to which this subsection applies includes the power to apply sections 170 to 173 to any description of property provided as cover for margin in relation to contracts in relation to which the power is exercised or, as the case may be, property subject to Charges in relation to which the power is exercised.

(2)

The Rules may provide that those sections apply with such exceptions, additions and adaptations as may be specified in the Rules.

(3)

Subsection (1) applies to the powers of the Regulator under sections 165, 169 and 179(1)(c).
