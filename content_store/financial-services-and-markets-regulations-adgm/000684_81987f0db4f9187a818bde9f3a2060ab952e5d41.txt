Non-ADGM Persons361

(1)

A Non-ADGM Person does not carry on an activity of the kind specified by paragraph 4 by—

(a)

entering into a transaction as principal with or through an Authorised Person, or an Exempt Person acting in the course of a business comprising a Regulated Activity in relation to which he is exempt; or

(b)

entering into a transaction as principal with a person in the Abu Dhabi Global Market, if the transaction is the result of a legitimate approach.

(2)

(a)
