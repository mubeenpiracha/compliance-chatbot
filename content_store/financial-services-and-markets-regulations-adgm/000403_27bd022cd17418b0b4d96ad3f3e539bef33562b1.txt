Corporates and Partnerships

222. Contraventions by bodies corporate etc.

(1)

If a contravention of these Regulations committed by a Body Corporate is shown—

(a)

to have been committed with the consent or connivance of an Officer; or

(b)

to be attributable to any neglect on his part;

the Officer as well as the Body Corporate commits the contravention and shall be liable to be proceeded against and punished accordingly.

(2)

If the affairs of a Body Corporate are managed by its members, subsection (1) applies in relation to the acts and defaults of a member in connection with his functions of management as if he were a Director of the Body Corporate.

(3)

If a contravention of these Regulations committed by a Partnership is shown—

(a)

to have been committed with the consent or connivance of a Partner; or

(b)

to be attributable to any neglect on his part;

the Partner as well as the Partnership commits the contravention and shall be liable to be proceeded against and punished accordingly.
