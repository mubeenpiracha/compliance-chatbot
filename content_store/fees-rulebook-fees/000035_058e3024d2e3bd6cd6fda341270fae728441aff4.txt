(b) $60,000 for all the Regulated Activities to be undertaken in relation to Virtual Assets

Guidance

1. These fees are in addition to any incurred for Regulated Activities carried on that do not involve Virtual Assets.

2. For additional guidance concerning the fees associated with Regulated Activities carried on in relation to Virtual Assets, please refer to the Regulator’s “Guidance – Regulation of Virtual Asset Activities in ADGM”.
