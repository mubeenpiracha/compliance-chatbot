3.10 Other Regulated Activities

3.10.1 An Applicant for a Financial Services Permission must pay to the Regulator an application

fee of $10,000 to carry on the Regulated Activity of:

(a) Arranging Credit;

(b) Operating a Multilateral Trading Facility;
