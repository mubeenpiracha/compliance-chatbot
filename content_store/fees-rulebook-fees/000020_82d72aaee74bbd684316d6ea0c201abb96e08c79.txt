3.5 Managing a Profit Sharing Investment Account

3.5.1 An Applicant for a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Managing a Profit Sharing Investment Account must pay to the Regulator an application fee of $30,000.

3.5.2 Subject to Rule 3.3.3(b), an Authorised Person with a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Managing a Profit Sharing Investment Account must pay to the Regulator an annual supervision fee of $30,000.
