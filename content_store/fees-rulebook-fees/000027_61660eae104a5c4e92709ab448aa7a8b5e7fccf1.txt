3.11 Effecting Contracts of Insurance and Carrying Out Contracts of Insurance

Insurers

3.11.1 An Applicant for a Financial Services Permission to carry on either one or both of the Regulated Activities of Effecting Contracts of Insurance or Carrying Out Contracts of Insurance as Principal must pay to the Regulator an application fee of $40,000 for either or both Regulated Activities.

3.11.2 An Authorised Person with a Financial Services Permission to carry on either one or both of the Regulated Activities of Effecting Contracts of Insurance or Carrying Out Contracts of Insurance as Principal must pay to the Regulator an annual supervision fee of $50,000 for either or both Regulated Activities.
