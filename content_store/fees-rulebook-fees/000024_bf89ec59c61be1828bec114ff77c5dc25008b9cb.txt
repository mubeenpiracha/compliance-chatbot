3.9A

Issuing a Fiat-Referenced Token

3.9A.1 An Applicant for a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Issuing a Fiat-Referenced Token must pay to the Regulator an application fee of $70,000.

3.9A.2 An Authorised Person with a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Issuing a Fiat-Referenced Token must pay to the Regulator an annual supervision fee of $70,000.
