20.

Cancellation of licence: procedure

(1)

If the Registrar proposes to cancel a licensed person's licence otherwise than at the person's request, it must give the person a warning notice.

(2)

If the Registrar decides to cancel a licensed person's licence otherwise than at the person's request, it must give the person a decision notice.

21.

Right to refer matters to the court

(1)

An applicant who is aggrieved by the determination of an application made under this Part may refer the matter to the court.

(2)

A licensed person who is aggrieved by the exercise by the Registrar of its own-initiative variation power or its own-initiative requirement power may refer the matter to the court.
