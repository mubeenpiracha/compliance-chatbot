 IP.

Application to sections 33 and 34

For the purposes of sections 33 and 34, if the person under investigation is not a licensed person the references in this Part of this Schedule to a licensed person are to be taken to be references to the person under investigation.

Part 2: Additional Rules

A person who is, or at the relevant time was, the partner, manager, employee, agent, banker, auditor, actuary or lawyer of -

(a)

the person under investigation ("A");

(b)

a parent undertaking of A;

(c)

a subsidiary undertaking of A;

(d)

a subsidiary undertaking of a parent undertaking of A; or

(e)

a parent undertaking of a subsidiary undertaking of A.
