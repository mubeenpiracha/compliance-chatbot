Simplified Customer Due Diligence

source of funds

Means the origin of funds which relate to a transaction or service and includes how such funds are connected to the source of wealth of a customer or Beneficial Owner.

source of wealth

Means how the global wealth or net worth of a customer or Beneficial Owner is or was acquired or accumulated.

Suspicious Activity Report (SAR)

Means a report regarding suspicious activity (including a suspicious transaction) made to the FIU under Federal Law No. 20 of 2018 and Cabinet Decision No. 10 of 2019.
