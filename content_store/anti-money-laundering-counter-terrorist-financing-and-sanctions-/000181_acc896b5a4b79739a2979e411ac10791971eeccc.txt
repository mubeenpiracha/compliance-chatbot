10.2 Relevant United Nations resolutions and sanctions

10.2.1

(1)

A Relevant Person must establish and maintain effective systems and controls to ensure that on an ongoing basis it is properly informed as to, and takes reasonable measures to comply with, relevant resolutions or sanctions issued by the United Nations Security Council.

(2)

The systems and controls in referred to in (1) must enable the Relevant Person to comply with the requirements in Article 21 of Cabinet Decision No. 74 of 2020.
