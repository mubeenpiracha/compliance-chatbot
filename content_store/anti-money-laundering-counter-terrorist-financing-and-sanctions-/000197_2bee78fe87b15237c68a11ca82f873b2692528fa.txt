11.3 Qualities of a MLRO

11.3.1 A Relevant Person must ensure that its MLRO:

(a)

is fit and proper, and has the competence and capability, to perform the MLRO role and has adequate knowledge and understanding of relevant AML legislation;

(b)

has direct access to its senior management;

(c)

has sufficient resources including, if necessary, an appropriate number of appropriately trained Employees to assist in the performance of his duties in an effective, objective and independent manner;

(d)

has a level of seniority and independence within the Relevant Person to enable him to act on his own authority; and

(e)

has timely and unrestricted access to information sufficient to enable him to carry out his responsibilities in Rule 11.4.1.
