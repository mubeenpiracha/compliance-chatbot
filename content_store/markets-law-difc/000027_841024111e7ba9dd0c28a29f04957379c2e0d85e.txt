CHAPTER 3: PROSPECTUS REQUIREMENT

14. Obligation to issue a Prospectus

(1)

A person shall not, subject to Article 14(3):

(a)

make an Offer of Securities to the Public in or from the DIFC;

(b)

have Securities admitted to trading on an Authorised Market Institution; or

(c)

have Security Tokens admitted to trading on an Alternative Trading System,

unless there is an Approved Prospectus in relation to the relevant Securities.
