CHAPTER 3 – MARKET DISCLOSURE

40.

Database

(1)

The DFSA shall establish and maintain an electronic data gathering, analysis and retrieval system (the “database”) for the receipt and storage of information filed or disclosed under this Part and any Rules made under this Part. The database is for the purpose of making information available to the public except where such information is confidential as prescribed in the Rules.

(2)

The DFSA may delegate to any person all or part of any function in Article 40(1) where it is satisfied that there are appropriate safeguards to ensure integrity and safety of the information and any applicable data protection requirements.
