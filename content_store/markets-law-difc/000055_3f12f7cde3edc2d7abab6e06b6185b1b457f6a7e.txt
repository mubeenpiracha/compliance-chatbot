CHAPTER 3 – RECOGNITION

37.

Recognised Status

(1)

The DFSA shall, by Rules, permit an Authorised Market Institution to admit as a member a recognised member.

(2)

A person which operates an exchange, clearing house or alternative trading system from a place of business outside the DIFC (the “operator”) shall not provide direct access to its facilities to persons in the DIFC unless such operator is admitted to the list of recognised persons maintained by the DFSA pursuant to Article 37(4).
