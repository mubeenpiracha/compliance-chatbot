(3)

A Fixed Penalty Notice must not be given 12 months after the day on which the contravention is alleged to have taken place.

(4)

The penalty specified in a Fixed Penalty Notice must not exceed $50,000 for a single contravention.

(5)

If a person is given a Fixed Penalty Notice:

(a)

no fine or censure may be imposed on the person under Articles 90(2)(a) or (b) for the alleged contravention before the end of the period prescribed under Article 91(8) for payment of the penalty; and

(b)

if the penalty has been paid and the contravention is no longer ongoing before the end of that period, no fine or censure may be imposed on the person under Articles 90(2)(a) or (b) for that contravention.

(6)

If the person to whom a Fixed Penalty Notice is given pays the penalty, the person is taken to have committed the contravention.

(7)

Articles 91(5) and (6) do not apply if a Fixed Penalty Notice has been withdrawn by the DFSA.

(8)
