The different types of Domestic Funds

Guidance

Article 13 of the Law defines Funds as either Domestic Funds or Foreign Funds. Article 15 of the Law then further divides Domestic Funds into three categories which are listed below in terms of the level of regulation (from most intensively regulated to least intensively regulated):

(a)

Public Funds;

(b)

Exempt Funds; and

(c)

Qualified Investor Funds.
