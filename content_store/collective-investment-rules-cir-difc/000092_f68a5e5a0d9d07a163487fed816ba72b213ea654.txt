A Fund Manager of an External Fund should be mindful that overarching obligations applicable to Authorised Firms continue to apply to them, including Article 22 of the Law, Article 38 of the Law, GEN chapter 5 (Systems and controls requirements) and GEN section 4.2 (The Principles for Authorised Firms). As an Authorised Firm, the Fund Manager would need to observe high standards of integrity and fair dealing, and apply due skill, care and diligence, in managing an External Fund.
