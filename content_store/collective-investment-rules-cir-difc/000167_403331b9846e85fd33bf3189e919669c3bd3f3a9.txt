Guidance

As Rule 8.12.2 (2) only applies in relation to a Public Fund, a Fund Manager of an Exempt Fund may make other appropriate arrangements in respect of the provision of Fund Administration.

Trustee

(1)

Subject to Rules 8.12.4, a Trustee may, with the prior written consent of the Fund Manager, delegate one or both of the Financial Service activities of Providing Fund Administration and Providing Custody to a Service Provider.

(2)

For the purposes of (1), and in relation to Providing Fund Administration for a Public Fund, the Service Provider must be:

(a)

a Person authorised by the DFSA to carry on the activity of Providing Fund Administration; or

(b)

a Person who is lawfully entitled in a Zone 1 jurisdiction or Recognised Jurisdiction to carry on in that jurisdiction the activities of:
