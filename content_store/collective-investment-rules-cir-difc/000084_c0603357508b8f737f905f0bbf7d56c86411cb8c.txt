COLLECTIVE INVESTMENT RULES (CIR)

Record keeping

A Fund Administrator must maintain records which are sufficient to show and explain transactions in relation to each of the specific activities and functions which are being provided to each Fund, in respect of Unitholders or potential Unitholders of the Fund as appropriate.

The records required under Rule 5.1.6 must be:

(a)

capable of demonstrating to the Governing Body of the relevant Fund that any accounts prepared comply with the applicable requirements in this module or any other applicable legislation;

(b)

retained by the Fund Administrator for at least 6 years from the date to which they relate;
