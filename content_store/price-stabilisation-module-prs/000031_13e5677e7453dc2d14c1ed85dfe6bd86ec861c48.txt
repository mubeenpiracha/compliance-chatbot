PRICE STABILISATION (PRS)

6

DUAL-LISTINGS AND PRICE STABILISATION ACTIVITIES

6.1 Application

6.1.1

(1)

This chapter applies to a Person who carries out Price Stabilisation of dual-listed Eligible Securities.

(2)

For the purposes of (1), “dual-listed Eligible Securities” are Eligible Securities which are listed concurrently on an Authorised Market Institution and on an exchange in a jurisdiction other than the DIFC.

Guidance

“Dual-listed Eligible Securities” in Rule 6.1.1(2) would, in relation to one listed security, include Certificates (e.g. global depository receipts) and Warrants over the other listed security.
