Application

6.1.1

(a)

This chapter applies in the case of a Domestic Fund:

(i)

which is an Islamic Fund, to its Fund Manager and where appointed, its Trustee; or

(ii)

which is an Umbrella Fund with one or more Islamic Sub-Funds, to its Fund Manager and where appointed, its Trustee in respect of those Sub- Funds.

(b)

The requirements that apply to a conventional Fund apply equally to an Islamic Fund, except as otherwise provided in this IFR 6.

(c)

In this IFR 6, except where otherwise provided, any reference to a Fund is to an Islamic Fund or to an Islamic Sub-Fund of an Umbrella Fund as the case may be and any reference to a Fund Manager is a reference to a Fund Manager of an Islamic Fund or a Fund Manager of an Islamic Sub-Fund of an Umbrella Fund.
