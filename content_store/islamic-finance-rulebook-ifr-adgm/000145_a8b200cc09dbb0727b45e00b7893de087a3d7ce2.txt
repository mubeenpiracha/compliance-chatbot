Specific disclosure for Takaful

8.2.1 Where an insurer or an insurance intermediary conducts Takaful with a Retail Client, the

disclosure for the purposes of COBS 6.7 must include:

(a)

the nature of the contracts between the Takaful Fund and the Takaful Operator;

(b)

the method of calculation of any fees or share of profits paid from the Takaful Fund to the Takaful Operator;

(c)

the basis on which any surpluses in the Takaful Fund will be shared; and

(d)

any circumstances in which additional contributions to the Takaful Fund may be required.
