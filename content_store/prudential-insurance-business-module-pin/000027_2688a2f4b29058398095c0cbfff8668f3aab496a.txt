APPLICATION

1.1

Application

1.1.1

Subject to Rule 1.1.2, this module (PIN) applies to every Insurer except to the extent that a provision specifies a narrower application.

Guidance

1.

An Insurer is defined in the GLO as an Authorised Firm which is authorised under its Licence to carry on in or from the DIFC, one or more of the Financial Services constituting Insurance Business.

2.

The Rules in PIN apply in relation to all activities of an Insurer, whether carried on within the DIFC or elsewhere.

1.1.2 Chapters 2, 3, 4, 6, 7 and 9 do not apply to an Insurer that is an Authorised ISPV,

unless expressly provided otherwise.
