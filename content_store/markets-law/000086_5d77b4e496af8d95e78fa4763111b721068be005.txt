(3)

A person shall not be found to have contravened Article 59 if:

(a)

the person establishes that the information was disclosed by him in accordance with any requirement of the law or a court order; or

(b)

the person establishes that he reasonably believed that the inside information had been disclosed to the market in accordance with this Law or the Rules.
