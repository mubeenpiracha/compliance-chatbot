Administration of the Law

This Law, and the Rules made for the purposes of this Law, are administered by the DFSA.

The DFSA powers to make Rules

(1)

The DFSA may make Rules for the purposes of this Law pursuant to the power conferred upon it under Article 23 of the Regulatory Law 2004.

(2) Without limiting the generality of Article 23 of the Regulatory Law 2004, the

DFSA shall make the following Rules:

(a)

in relation to the offer of Securities or Crypto Tokens in or from the DIFC;

(b)

in relation to the licensing and supervision of Authorised Market Institutions, including requirements and procedures in the event of default by members of an Authorised Market Institution;
