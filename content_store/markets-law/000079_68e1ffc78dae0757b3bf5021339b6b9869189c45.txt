61. Misuse of information

A person shall not, in the DIFC or elsewhere, engage in any activity or conduct in relation to Investments or Crypto Tokens, which does not fall under Articles 58, 59 or 60:

(a)

by using information which is not generally available to market participants which, if available to a market participant, would be, or would be likely to be, regarded by him as relevant when deciding the terms on which transactions in Investments or Crypto Tokens should be effected; and
