12.2

Fund Manager and Trustee general duties and functions

12.2.1

(1) A Fund Manager shall:

(a)

manage the Fund including the Fund Property in accordance with the Fund's Constitution and its most recent Prospectus;

(b)

perform the functions conferred on it by the Fund's Constitution and by or under these Rules;

(c)

comply with any conditions or restrictions imposed by the Regulator including those on its Financial Services Permission or in respect of the Fund; and

(d)

comply with any requirements or limitations imposed under these Rules including any limits relating to financial interests it or any of its associates may hold in a Fund, for which it acts as the appointed Fund Manager.
