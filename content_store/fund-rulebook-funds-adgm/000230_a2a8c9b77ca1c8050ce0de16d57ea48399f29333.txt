Guidance

Regardless of who holds title to Fund Property, the Fund Manager must always ensure that, in accordance with Rule 12.2.2(1)(f), Fund Property is clearly identified as such and held separately from property of the Fund Manager and any other Funds.

16.

ACCOUNTING, AUDIT AND PERIODIC REPORTING OF A FUND

16.1

Application

16.1.1

(1) This Chapter applies to a Fund Manager and a Trustee of a Domestic Fund.

(2) In this Chapter, references to a "Fund" mean a "Domestic Fund".
