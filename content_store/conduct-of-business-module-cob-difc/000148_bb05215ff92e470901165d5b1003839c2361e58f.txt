Exercise of Discretion

5.2.5 Where a Trust Service Provider is responsible for exercising discretion for, or in relation to, its Clients, it must take all reasonable steps to obtain sufficient information in order to exercise, subject to Rule 5.2.6, its discretion or other powers in a proper manner.
