Transparency and disclosure

Policies and procedures

(1)

A Credit Rating Agency must, subject to (2), have adequate policies, procedures and controls to ensure that it discloses in a timely manner:

(a)

its Credit Ratings and any updates thereof;

(b)

its policies for distributing Credit Ratings and updates thereof;

(c)

the methodologies and models used and key assumptions made in preparing its Credit Ratings and any updates thereof; and

(d)

any other significant element relating to (a), (b) or (c) above.

(2)

A Credit Rating Agency is not required to disclose information where the information is subject to confidentiality requirements.
