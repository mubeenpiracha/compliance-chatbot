6.15 Advising on or Arranging Direct Long-Term Insurance

6.15.1

If an Authorised Firm advises on or arranges Direct Long-Term Insurance for a Retail Client, it must disclose to the Client:

(a)

the method of calculation of any bonuses;

(b)

an indication of surrender values and paid-up values, and the extent to which any such values are guaranteed;

(c)

for unit-linked insurance contracts, definition of the units to which they are linked, and a description of the underlying assets;

(d)

the basis of any projections included in the information; and

(e)

any facts that are material to the decision to invest, including any risks associated with the investment and factors that may adversely affect the performance of the investments.
