15.6.10 An Authorised Firm must:

(a)

not provide a Credit Facility to a Retail Client in connection with trading in Crypto Token Derivatives; and

(b)

take reasonable steps to ensure that a Retail Client does not use a credit card or third party credit facility to pay a Margin relating to a Crypto Token Derivative.

15.7 Technology and Governance Requirements

15.7.1 This section applies to an Authorised Firm that uses DLT or similar technology to

provide any of the following services:

(a)

Operate a MTF for Crypto Tokens;

(b)

hold or control Client Crypto Tokens; or

(c)

carry on any other Financial Service relating to Crypto Tokens.
