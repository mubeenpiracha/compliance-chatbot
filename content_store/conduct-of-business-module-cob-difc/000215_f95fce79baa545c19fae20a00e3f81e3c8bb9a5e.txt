An Authorised Firm must be able to demonstrate to the DFSA’s satisfaction the grounds upon which it considers the third party to be suitable to hold Client’s Collateral.

6.13.7

(1)

An Authorised Firm must take reasonable steps to ensure that the Collateral is properly safeguarded.

(2)

An Authorised Firm must withdraw the Collateral from the third party where the Collateral is not being properly safeguarded unless the Client has indicated otherwise in writing.
