Guidance

1.

An ATS Operator should have an effective surveillance system in place for:

a.

the coordinated surveillance of all activity on or through its facilities and activity in related Investments or Crypto Tokens conducted elsewhere; and

b.

communicating information about Market Abuse or suspected abuse, to the DFSA or appropriate regulatory authorities.
