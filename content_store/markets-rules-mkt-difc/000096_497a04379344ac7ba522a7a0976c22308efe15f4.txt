(2)

The DFSA may also require the appointment of a paying agent during the offer period.

(3)

The procedures in Schedule 3 to the Regulatory Law apply to a decision of the DFSA under this Rule.

(4)

If the DFSA decides to exercise its power under this Rule, the Offeror may refer the matter to the FMT for review.
