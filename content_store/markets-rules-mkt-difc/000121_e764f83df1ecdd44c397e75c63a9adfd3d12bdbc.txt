3.5 Related party transactions

Application

3.5.1 This section applies, subject to Rule 3.5.4, to:

(a)

a Reporting Entity; and

(b)

a Related Party of such a Reporting Entity.

Definitions

3.5.2

In this section, unless otherwise provided:

(a)

a Person is a Related Party of a Reporting Entity if that Person:

(i)

is, or was within the 12 months before the date of the Related Party Transaction:

(A)

a Director or a Person involved in the senior management of the Reporting Entity or a member of its Group;

(B)

an Associate of a Person referred to in (a)(i)(A); or
