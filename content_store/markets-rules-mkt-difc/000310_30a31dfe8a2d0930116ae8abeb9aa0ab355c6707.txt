Shares in public hands

9.7.2

(1)

A Listed Entity must ensure that a sufficient number of its Shares are distributed to the public at all times.

(2)

A Listed Entity which no longer complies with (1) must notify the DFSA as soon as possible after it first becomes aware of its non-compliance.
