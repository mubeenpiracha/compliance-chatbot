Definition of a Small or Medium-Sized Enterprise (SME)

1.3.3

(1)

An Applicant is a SME under this module if:

(a) it is applying for the admission of its Shares to the List; and

(b) the aggregate market value of all of its listed Shares on admission is

reasonably expected to be less than $250 million.

(2)

A Listed Entity is a SME under this module if:

(a) the aggregate market value of all of its listed Shares on admission was

less than $250 million; and

(b) it has not ceased to be a SME under (3).

(3)

A Listed Entity ceases to be a SME for the purposes of (2)(b), if the average aggregate market value of its listed Shares has exceeded $500 million for 90 consecutive days.
