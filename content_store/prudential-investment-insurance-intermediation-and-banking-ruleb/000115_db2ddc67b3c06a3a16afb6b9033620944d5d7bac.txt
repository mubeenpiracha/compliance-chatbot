Category 4

1.3.6

An Authorised Person is in Category 4 if:

(a)

its Financial Services Permission authorises it to carry on one or more of the Regulated Activities of Arranging Credit, Arranging Deals in Investments, Advising on Investments or Credit, Arranging Custody, Insurance Intermediation, Providing Trust Services (where it is not acting as trustee in respect of an express trust), Insurance Management, Acting as the Administrator of a Collective Investment Fund, Operating a Multilateral Trading Facility or Organised Trading Facility, Operating a Private Financing Platform or Providing Third Party Services; and

(b)

it does not meet the criteria of Categories 1, 2, 3A, 3B, 3C or 5.
