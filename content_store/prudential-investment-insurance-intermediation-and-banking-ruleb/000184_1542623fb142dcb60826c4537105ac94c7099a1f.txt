CET1 Deductions - intangible assets

3.10.8

For the purposes of Rule 3.10.7(b), an Authorised Person must determine the intangible assets to be deducted in accordance with the following:

(a)

the amount to be deducted must be reduced by the amount of associated deferred tax liabilities that would be extinguished if the intangible assets became impaired or were derecognised under the International Financial Reporting Standards; and

(b)

the amount to be deducted must include goodwill included in the valuation of significant Investments of the Authorised Person.
