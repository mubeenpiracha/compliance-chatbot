Foreign Exchange Risk Capital Requirement

5.6.1

An Authorised Person in Category 1, 2, 3A or 5 must, subject to Rule 5.6.2, calculate its in respect of Trading Book and Foreign Exchange Risk Capital Requirement Non-Trading Book foreign exchange positions by:

(a)

applying its internal Market Risk model which has been approved by the Regulator for this purpose; or

(b)

applying the Rules in Section A6.4.

5.6.2

An Authorised Person need not calculate a Foreign Exchange Risk Capital Requirement if:
