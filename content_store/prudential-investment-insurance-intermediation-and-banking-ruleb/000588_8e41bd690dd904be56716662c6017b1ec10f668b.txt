Restrictions on Category 3A, 3B, 3C and 4 Authorised Persons

8.5.2

(1)

An Authorised Person in Category 3A, 3B, 3C or 4 must not, subject to (2), be a Parent of an entity that:

(a)

is an Authorised Person in Category 1 or 5 or an Insurer; or

(b)

carries on activities that would, if conducted in the ADGM, constitute Accepting Deposits or Managing a Profit Sharing Investment Account which is a PSIAu.

(2)

An Authorised Person in Category 3A, 3B, 3C or 4 may own or control an entity referred to in (1) where it is itself a Subsidiary of:

(a)
