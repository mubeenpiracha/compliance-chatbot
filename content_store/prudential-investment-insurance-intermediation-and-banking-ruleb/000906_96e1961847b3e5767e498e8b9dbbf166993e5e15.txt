An Authorised Person should be able to measure its Market Risk Exposure both across risk types, such as interest rate, foreign exchange and commodities, and across the entire portfolio.

Where an Authorised Person is a member of a Group, which is subject to consolidated supervision, the Group should be able to monitor Market Risk Exposures on a consolidated basis (Chapter 8).

An Authorised Person should have the capability to assess the impact of any new transaction on its Market Risk position on an on-going basis, and should be capable of carrying out a full measurement of its positions at least daily.
