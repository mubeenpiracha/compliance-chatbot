Substandard

Doubtful

Criteria Includes Exposures with no element of uncertainty about timely repayment of the outstanding amounts, including principal and interest. Exposures are currently in regular payment status with prompt payments. Includes Exposures with deteriorating or potentially deteriorating credit quality which, may adversely affect the borrower's ability to make scheduled payments on time. The Exposures in this category warrant close attention by the Authorised Person. Includes Exposures which exhibit definitive deterioration in credit quality and impaired debt servicing capacity of the borrower. Includes Exposures which show strong credit quality deterioration, worse than those in substandard category, to the extent that the prospect of full recovery of all the outstanding amounts from the credit is questionable the probability of a credit loss is high, though the exact amount of loss cannot be determined yet.
