3.13

Minority interests and instruments issued by Subsidiaries

Minority interests that qualify for inclusion in consolidated CET1 Capital

3.13.1 Minority interests must include the CET1 Capital instruments, plus the related retained earnings and Share premium accounts of a Subsidiary, only where all of the following conditions are met:

(a)

the Subsidiary is one of the following:

(i)

an Authorised Person; or

(ii)

a regulated entity;

(b)

the Subsidiary is a member of the Financial Group and included in the scope of consolidated supervision in accordance with Chapter 8; and

(c)

those CET1 Capital instruments are owned by Persons other than the Undertakings included in the Financial Group.

3.13.2 Minority interests that are funded, directly or indirectly, through a Special Purpose Entity (SPE) or otherwise, by the Parent of the Authorised Person or any member of its Financial Group must not qualify for inclusion in the consolidated CET1 Capital of the Financial Group.
