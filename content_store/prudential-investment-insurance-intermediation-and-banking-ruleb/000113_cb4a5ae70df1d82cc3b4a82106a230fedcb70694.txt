Category 3C

1.3.5

An Authorised Person is in Category 3C if:

(a)

its Financial Services Permission authorises it to carry on one or more of the Regulated Activities of:

(i) Managing Assets;

(ii) Managing a Collective Investment Fund;

(iii) Providing Custody (where it does so other than for a Fund);

(iv) Managing a Profit Sharing Investment Account which is a PSIAr;

(v) Providing Trust Services (where it is acting as trustee in respect of at least one

express trust);

(vi) Providing Money Services; or

(vii) Issuing a Fiat-Referenced Token; and

(b)

it does not meet the criteria of Categories 1, 2, 3A, 3B or 5.
