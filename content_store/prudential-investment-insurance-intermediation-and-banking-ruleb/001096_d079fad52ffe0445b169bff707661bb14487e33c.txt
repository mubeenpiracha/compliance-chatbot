Guidance

1.

The requirements in Rules A10.2.2 to A10.2.4 are intended to ensure that the stock of HQLA is managed in such a way that the Authorised Person can, and is able to demonstrate that it can, immediately use the assets as a source of contingent funds that is available to convert into cash to fill funding gaps between cash inflows and outflows at any time during the 30-day stress period, with no restriction on the use of the liquidity generated.
