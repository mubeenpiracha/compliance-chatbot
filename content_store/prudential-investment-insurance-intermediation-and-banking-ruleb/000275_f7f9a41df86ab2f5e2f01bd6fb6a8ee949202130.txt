CREDIT RISK

Introduction

Guidance

1.

Chapter 4 deals with the prudential requirements relating to the management of Credit Risk by an Authorised Person. Credit Risk refers to risk of incurring losses due to failure on the part of a borrower or a Counterparty to fulfil their obligations in respect of a financial transaction.

2.

This Chapter aims to ensure that an Authorised Person holds sufficient regulatory capital of acceptable quality so that it can absorb unexpected losses arising out of its Credit Risk Exposures, should the need arise and that it continues to operate in a sustainable manner.
