Equity Futures and forwards

A6.3.13 An Authorised Person must treat a Future or forward on a single equity as a notional position in the underlying equity. In addition, an interest rate leg must be included in the interest rate risk calculation in Section 5.4 as a notional government security.

A6.3.14 An Authorised Person must treat a Future or forward on a single country equity index
