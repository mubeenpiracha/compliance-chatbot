A7.3.2

Subject to Rule A7.3.4, an Authorised Person which uses the Alternative Standardised Approach must calculate its Operational Risk Capital Requirement for its retail banking business line according to the following formula:

KRB = BRB × m × LARB

where:

KRB =

the Capital Requirement for the retail banking business line

BRB =

the beta factor for the retail banking business line

LARB =

total outstanding retail loans and advances (non-risk weighted and gross of provisions), averaged over the past three years
