If an Authorised Person:

(i)

has a material change in its expenditure (whether up or down); or

(ii)

has varied its authorised activities,

it must recalculate its Annual Audited Expenditure and Expenditure Based Capital Minimum accordingly.

(b)

Where an Authorised Person has recalculated its Annual Audited Expenditure and Expenditure Based Capital Minimum in accordance with (a), it must submit this recalculation to the Regulator within seven days of its completion and seek agreement/approval from the Regulator. The Regulator may within thirty days of receiving the recalculation object to the recalculation and require the Authorised Person to revise its Expenditure Based Capital Minimum.
