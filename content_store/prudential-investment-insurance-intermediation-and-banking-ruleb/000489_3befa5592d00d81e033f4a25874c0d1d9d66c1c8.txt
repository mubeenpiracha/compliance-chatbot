Guidance

Guidance in respect of what an Authorised Person's Market Risk policy should include is provided in Section A6.1.

5.3

Calculation of the Market Risk Capital Requirement

5.3.1

An Authorised Person must calculate its Market Risk Capital Requirement as the sum of the following components:

(a)

Interest Rate Risk Capital Requirement;

(b)

Equity Risk Capital Requirement;

(c)

Foreign Exchange Risk Capital Requirement;

(d)

Commodities Risk Capital Requirement;
