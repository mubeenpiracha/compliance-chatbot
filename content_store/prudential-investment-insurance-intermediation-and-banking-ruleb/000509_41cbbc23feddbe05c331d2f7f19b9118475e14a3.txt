Application

6.1.1

This Chapter applies to an Authorised Person as follows:

(a)

Sections 6.1 to 6.9 apply to an Authorised Person in any Category;

(b)

Sections 6.10 and 6.11 apply only to an Authorised Person in Category 1, 2, 3A or 5;

(c)

Section 6.12 applies only to an Authorised Person in Category 3B, 3C or 4.
