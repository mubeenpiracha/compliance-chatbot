(3)

An Authorised Person must submit to the Regulator monthly Regulatory Returns, in the applicable format prescribed by the Regulator, within fifteen days of the end of the reporting period to which the Regulatory Return relates.

2.3.8

(1)

When a Regulatory Return which is required under these Rules is submitted after the due date, such non-submission incurs an administrative fee as prescribed in the FEES Rulebook.

(2)

Nothing in this Rule limits the right of the Regulator to take any other action.
