5.4

Interest Rate Risk Capital Requirement

5.4.1

An Authorised Person in Category 1 or 2 must calculate its Interest Rate Risk Capital Requirement in respect of Trading Book transactions:

(a)

by applying its internal Market Risk model which has been approved by the Regulator for this purpose; or

(b)

by applying the Rules set out in Section A6.2.
