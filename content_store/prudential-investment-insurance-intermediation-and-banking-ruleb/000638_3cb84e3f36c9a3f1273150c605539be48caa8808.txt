DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS

Introduction

Guidance

1.

This Chapter specifies the disclosures required to be made by an Authorised Person to enable market participants and potential Counterparties to exercise market discipline in relation to the firm. Chapter 11 applies to banks, principal dealers and Islamic banks and specifies the disclosure requirements for such firms.

2.

App12 provides a detailed list of disclosure requirements for Authorised Persons to which this Chapter applies.
