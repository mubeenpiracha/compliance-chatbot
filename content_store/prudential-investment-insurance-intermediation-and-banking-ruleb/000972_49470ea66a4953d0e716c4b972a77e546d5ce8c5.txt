An Authorised Person may net positions in different commodities where those commodities:

(a)

are deliverable against each other; and

(b)

are in one or more sub-categories of the same category;

Guidance

1.

For the purposes of Rule A6.5.4, an example of a category is oil. An example of a sub-category is Brent.

2.

For the Simplified Approach and the Maturity Ladder approach, long and short positions in each commodity may be reported on a net basis for the purposes of calculating open positions.
