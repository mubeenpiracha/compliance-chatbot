Guidance

The gross income is measured for each business line, not the whole institution, i.e. in corporate finance, the indicator is the gross income generated in the corporate finance business line.

A7.3

Alternative Standardised Approach

A7.3.1

An Authorised Person which uses the Alternative Standardised Approach must calculate in accordance with the Standardised Approach in Section A7.2 as modified by replacing annual gross income for its retail banking and commercial banking business lines with loans and advances multiplied by a fixed factor 'm'.
