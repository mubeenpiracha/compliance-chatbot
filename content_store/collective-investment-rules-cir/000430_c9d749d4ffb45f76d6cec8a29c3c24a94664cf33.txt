Application

15.1.1

Only Rules 15.1.1A, 15.1.2, 15.1.4, 15.1.5(a), 15.1.8, 15.1.9, 15.1.10 and 15.1.11 apply to an Offer of a Unit of a Foreign Fund that is a Passported Fund where the DIFC is the Host Jurisdiction.

Clients to whom Offers of Units of Foreign Funds can be made

15.1.1A An Authorised Firm must not, in or from the DIFC, Offer a Unit of a Foreign Fund to a Retail Client unless the Units of the Foreign Fund can be offered, under the home jurisdiction regulation applying to that Fund, to retail investors.

Prospectus disclosure relating to Foreign Funds

15.1.2 Where an Authorised Firm Offers a Unit of a Foreign Fund to a Person, it must make available to that Person a copy of a current Prospectus relating to the Fund which complies with:

(a)

the additional requirements in Rule 15.1.3 at the time of the Offer; or

(b)

for a Passported Fund, the relevant requirements of the Home Jurisdiction, including its relevant Fund Protocol rules or regulations.
