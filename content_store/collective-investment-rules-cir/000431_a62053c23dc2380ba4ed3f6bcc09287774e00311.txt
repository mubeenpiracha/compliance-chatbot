Guidance

Under Article 50(3)(c) of the Law, a Prospectus includes, in the case of a Foreign Fund the Units of which are marketed in or from the DIFC, any prospectus or other disclosure document prepared in accordance with the laws applicable to that Foreign Fund.

15.1.3

(1)

The Prospectus of a Foreign Fund made available by an Authorised Firm must be in the English language.

(2)

The Prospectus must contain in a prominent position, or have attached to it, a statement that clearly:

(a)

describes the foreign jurisdiction and the legislation in that jurisdiction that applies to the Fund;

(b)

states the name of the relevant Financial Services Regulator in that jurisdiction;

(c)

describes the regulatory status accorded to the Fund by that Regulator;

(d)

includes the following warning:

“This Prospectus relates to a Fund which is not subject to any form of regulation or approval by the Dubai Financial Services Authority (“DFSA”).
