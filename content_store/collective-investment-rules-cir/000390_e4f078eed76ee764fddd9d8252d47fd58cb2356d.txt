A Prospectus may consist of a single document, or a multi-part Prospectus containing:

(a)

information relating to the Fund Manager and Trustee; and

(c)

information relating to the Fund.

If a Prospectus is a multi-part Prospectus, the Fund Manager must ensure that the Prospectus as a whole is up-to-date.
