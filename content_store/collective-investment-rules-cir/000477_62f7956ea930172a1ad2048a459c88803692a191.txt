APP 3 APPROVALS AND NOTIFICATIONS

A 3.1

Alterations to a Fund

A 3.1.1

(1)

The Fund Manager of a Public Fund must, by way of a Special Resolution, obtain prior approval from the Unitholders for any proposed change to the Domestic Fund which is a fundamental change.
