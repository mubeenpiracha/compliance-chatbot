In (4), if the Fund Manager and if appointed the Trustee agree that the receipt of the property concerned for the account of the Fund:

(a)

is not likely to result in any material prejudice to the interest of the Unitholders of the Fund;

(b)

is consistent with the objectives of the Fund or Sub-Fund of an Umbrella Fund; and

(c)

could be effected without any breach of the requirements relating to the borrowing and investment restrictions relating to the Fund or Sub-Fund in chapter 10.5;

then the transfer may be effective and the issue of Units in exchange for assets as part of a transfer scheme may be undertaken.
