(3)

The Prospectus must, except as provided in (4), be in the English language.

(4)

The Key Investor Information Document (KIID) for a Passported Fund must be in both Arabic and English.

(5)

The expiry date of a Prospectus must be no later than 12 months after the date of the Prospectus.

(6)

A reference to a Director in this Rule includes the Corporate Director of an Investment Company, and the individual directors of that Corporate Director.
