9.4.11

If the reassessment referred to in Rule 9.4.9 shows that the amount of assets made available is less than the amount that the insurer is required to make available, the insurer must, within two months of the effective date of the reassessment, make additional assets available so that the Insurer complies with the requirements of the collateral notice.

9.4.12

If the reassessment shows that the amount of assets made available is more than the amount that the insurer is required to make available, the Insurer may, with the written consent of the DFSA, remove assets from those made available provided that the Insurer complies with the requirements of the collateral notice after the assets have been removed.
