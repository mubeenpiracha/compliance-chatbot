A5.2 Adjusted non-cellular capital resources

A5.2.1 An Insurer must calculate its Adjusted Non-Cellular Capital Resources according to

the formula:

ANCR = ANE – HNCA

ANCR means the Insurer’s Adjusted Non-Cellular Capital Resources;
