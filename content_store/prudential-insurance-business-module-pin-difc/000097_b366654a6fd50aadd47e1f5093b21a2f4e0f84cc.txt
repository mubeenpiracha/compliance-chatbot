FINANCIAL AND OTHER REPORTING BY INSURERS

6.1

Introduction

6.1.1

This chapter applies to all Insurers.

Guidance

6.2

Annual regulatory return

6.2.1

An Insurer must, at the end of each reporting period, prepare an Annual Regulatory Return.

6.2.2

The Annual Regulatory Return must comprise the statements set out in App10, together with any Supplementary Notes pertaining to those forms, and including a Statement by Directors.
