Guidance

This section contains provisions that enable the DFSA to require an Insurer that is in run-off or going into run-off to post collateral assets or make equivalent arrangements by letter of credit, to support the Insurance Liabilities and Minimum Capital Requirements applicable to the Insurer. This section applies only to an Insurer that:
