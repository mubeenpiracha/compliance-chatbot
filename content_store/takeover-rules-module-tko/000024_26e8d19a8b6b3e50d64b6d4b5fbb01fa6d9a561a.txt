Guidance

Where information is disclosed in confidence to a Bidder or Target, a Director may receive material information in his capacity as Director. That Director, or his associates, may also hold shares in the Bidder or Target in a personal capacity. In these circumstances, a Director should be mindful of his duties, including in relation to conflicts of interests and misuse of position. Directors should also be well aware of restrictions and prohibitions under the Markets Law 2012 including Article 42 relating to insider dealing.
