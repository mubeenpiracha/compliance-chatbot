3.4.3 A disclosure pursuant to Rule 3.4.1 must, at least:

(a)

provide the total number of the relevant Securities purchased or sold;

(b)

provide the total number of the respective dealings for the Person’s own account and for the account of clients;

(c)

detail the prices paid or received;

(d)

identify the Person dealing.
