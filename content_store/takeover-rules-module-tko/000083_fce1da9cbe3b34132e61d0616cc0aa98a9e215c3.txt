8.2.2 The Governing Body of the Target must circulate its views on the Bid, including any

alternative Bids, to its shareholders in a Target Circular.

Guidance

Nothing in these Rules precludes a Bidder and a Target issuing a joint document under which they comply with their respective obligations.

8.2.3

In its initial Target Circular, the Governing Body of the Target must disclose the information specified in App2 (whether recommending acceptance or rejection of the Bid), together with any other information it considers to be relevant to enable its shareholders to reach a properly informed decision on the Bid.
