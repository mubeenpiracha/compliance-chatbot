RESTRICTED DEALINGS BEFORE AND DURING AN OFFER PERIOD

3.1 Acquisitions before a bid period

3.1.1 Subject to Rules 4.3.1 and 5.2.1, when a Bidder or any Person acting in concert with it

has acquired Shares in the Target:

(a)

within the six month period prior to the commencement of the Bid Period; or

(b)

during the period, if any, between the commencement of the Bid Period and an announcement made by the Bidder in accordance with Section 2.4;

the Bid to the shareholders of the same class must not be on less favourable terms.
