1.5.7 At no time after a bona fide Bid has been communicated to the Governing Body of a Target, or after the Governing Body of a Target has reason to believe that such a Bid might be imminent, may any action be taken by the Governing Body of the Target, without the approval of the shareholders in general meeting, which could effectively result in any bona fide Bid being frustrated or in the shareholders being denied an opportunity to decide on its merits.
