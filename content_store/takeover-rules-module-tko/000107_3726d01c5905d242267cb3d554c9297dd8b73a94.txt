11.3 Settlement of Consideration

11.3.1 The consideration must be paid within 14 days of the date of the Bid becoming or

being declared unconditional and acceptance thereof, whichever is the later.

11.4 Revision of bids

11.4.1 A Bidder must ensure that its Bid, if revised, is kept open for at least 14 days following

the date on which the revised Bid Document is posted.
