5.2 Requirement for a bid in cash

5.2.1 Where the Shares of any class under Bid in the Target acquired for cash by a Bidder or any Person acting in concert with it during the Bid Period, or within six months prior to its commencement, carry 10% or more of the voting rights currently exercisable at a class meeting of that class, the Bid for that class must be in cash or accompanied by a cash alternative at not less than the highest price paid by the Bidder or any Person acting in concert with it for Shares of that class during the Bid Period or within six months prior to its commencement.
