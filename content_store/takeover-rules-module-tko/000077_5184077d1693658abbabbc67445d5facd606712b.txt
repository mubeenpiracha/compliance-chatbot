7.2 Distribution of Documents and Announcements

7.2.1 Copies of all relevant documents and announcements bearing on a Bid, and of advertisements and any material to be released to the media, must at the time of release be filed with the DFSA.

Guidance

The time of filing of Bid Documents and Target Circulars with the DFSA is addressed in Section 11.1.
