Guidance

In exceptional cases, the DFSA may waive or modify the application of the Rules if it is satisfied that circumstances are appropriate. See Chapter 16 of these Rules. For example, the DFSA may, in appropriate circumstances, waive the application of the Rules in relation to a Person who is a Reporting Entity solely by reason of the DFSA having declared that the Person is a Reporting Entity.

1.3 Regulatory proceedings in the FMT

Guidance

Article 70(1)(c) of the Markets Law 2012 provides that the FMT has jurisdiction to hear and determine regulatory proceedings in relation to an issue arising out of a takeover, takeover offer, merger or acquisition of shares. Article 29(3) of the Regulatory Law sets out who may bring a regulatory proceeding before the FMT.
