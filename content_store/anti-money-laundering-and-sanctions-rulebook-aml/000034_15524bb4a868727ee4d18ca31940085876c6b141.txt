Means a Person, other than a Recognised Body, who is authorised under the FSMR.

Means, in relation to a customer, a Natural Person who ultimately owns or controls the customer or a Natural Person on whose behalf a transaction is conducted or a business relationship is established and includes:

(a)

in relation to a body corporate, a Person referred to in Rule 8.3.3(2);

(b)

in relation to a Partnership, a Person referred to in Rule 8.3.4(2);

(c)

in relation to a trust or other similar Legal Arrangement, a Person referred to in Rule 8.3.5(2); and

(d)

in relation to a foundation, a Person referred to in Rule 8.3.6(2).

Means any body corporate, including limited liability partnership and a body corporate constituted under the law of a country or territory outside of ADGM.

Means a Retail Client, Professional Client or Market Counterparty as defined in COBS 2.

Means an agreement between an Authorised Person and a Client which is made or entered into in accordance with COBS 3.3.
