Group Recovery Plans

The Regulator may require an ADGM Parent Institution to prepare and submit to the Regulator a Group Recovery Plan for the Group headed by the ADGM Parent Institution as a whole.

The Group Recovery Plan shall identify measures that may be required to be implemented at the level of the ADGM Parent Institution, the Institutions and other Subsidiaries, with the aim to achieve the stabilisation of the Group as a whole, or any Institution of the Group, when it
