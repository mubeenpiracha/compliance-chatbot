Conditions for application of a Resolution Tool

(1)

When applying a Resolution Tool to an Institution, the Regulator shall take into account the measures provided in the Institution's Resolution Plan (if applicable) unless the Regulator assesses that, in the circumstances, the Resolution Objectives may be achieved more effectively by taking actions that are not provided in the Resolution Plan.
