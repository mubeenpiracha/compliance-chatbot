AUTHORISED MARKET INSTITUTIONS (AMI)

PART 3:

LICENSING REQUIREMENTS

4.

GENERAL

4.1

Application

4.1.1

This chapter applies to a Person who is an Authorised Market Institution or an applicant for such a Licence.

4.2

Licensing Requirements

4.2.1

(1)

An Authorised Market Institution must have adequate arrangements both at the time a Licence is granted and at all times thereafter to meet the applicable Licensing Requirements as specified in (2).
