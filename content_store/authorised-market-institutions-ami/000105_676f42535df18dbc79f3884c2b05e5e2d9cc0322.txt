(3)

An Authorised Market Institution which seeks to dispense with public consultation on the ground referred to in (1)(b) must lodge with the DFSA a statement setting out:

(a)

the text of both the proposed amendment and the Business Rules that are to be amended; and

(b)

(i)

(ii)

that it had taken into account the views and interests of its Members and other stakeholders as appropriate in developing the proposed amendment.
