AUTHORISED MARKET INSTITUTIONS (AMI)

(1)

A Person who has been approved by the DFSA as a Controller of an Authorised Market Institution subject to any conditions must comply with the relevant conditions of approval.

(2)

A Person who has been notified by the DFSA pursuant to Rule 8.2.4(2)(c) as an unacceptable Controller must not proceed with the proposed acquisition of control of the Authorised Market Institution.
