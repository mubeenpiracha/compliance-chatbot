The DFSA shall, by Rules, prescribe the requirements which an application for registration must meet before such an application can be accepted by the DFSA.

29.

Providing information in relation to the application

(1)

The DFSA may require the Fund Manager, and if relevant the Trustee, to provide additional information reasonably required for the DFSA to be able to make a decision with regard to the application.

(2)

If, at any time between the filing of an application for registration and the grant of a registration, the Fund Manager or, if appointed, the Trustee becomes aware of any material change, error or omission reasonably likely to be relevant to the application under consideration, it shall inform the DFSA in writing of such change without delay.
