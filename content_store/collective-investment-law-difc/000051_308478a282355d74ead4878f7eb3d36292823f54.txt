30.

Rejection of an application

(1)

The DFSA may refuse to grant an application for registration of a Public Fund if it is not satisfied that the requirements referred to in Article 28 have been met.

(2)

The procedures in Schedule 3 to the Regulatory Law 2004 apply to a decision of the DFSA under Article 30(1).

(3)

If the DFSA decides to exercise its power under Article 30(1), the applicant may refer the matter to the FMT for review.
