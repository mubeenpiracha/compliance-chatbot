Ijarah/Ijarah Muntahia Bittamleek

Asset with an Authorised Person available for lease before purchase by the Counterparty – for both contracts with both binding or non-binding promise to lease

Residential real estate where the lessee has the right to purchase property at the end of the lease and the lessor has a legally enforceable first charge over the property

Total estimated value of lease receivables for the whole duration of the Ijarah, less any recovery value of the leased asset
