Managing PSIAs (which may be either a Restricted PSIA or an Unrestricted PSIA) is determined in accordance with PRU Rule 1.3. An Authorised Person which Manages PSIAs (whether as an Islamic Financial Institution or through an Islamic Window) must comply with the requirements in PRU in relation to specific prudential requirements relating to Trading Book and Non-Trading Book activities, including Credit Risk, Market Risk, Liquidity Risk and Group Risk.
