Guidance

(i)

The Regulator considers that this Guidance will assist an Authorised Person in applying the appropriate risk weighting or capital charge to each Islamic Contract for the purpose of IFR 5.4.7. Accordingly, the Regulator expects an Authorised Person managing PSIAs, which are Unrestricted PSIAs to pay due regard to this Guidance.

(ii)

The rules in this IFR 5 and this Guidance are also relevant to an Authorised Person which invests in or holds Islamic Contracts, when calculating CRCOM for Islamic Contracts under PRU Rule 4.
