155. Particulars of directors to be registered: corporate directors and firms

A company’s register of directors must contain the following particulars in the case of a body corporate, or a firm that is a legal person under the law by which it is governed–

(a)

corporate or firm name,

(b)

registered or principal office,

(c)

particulars of–

(i)

the legal form of the company or firm and the law by which it is governed, and

(ii)

if applicable, the register in which it is entered (including details of the state) and its registration number in that register.

157. Duty to notify Registrar of changes7

(1)

A company must, within the period of 14 days from–

(a)

a person becoming or ceasing to be a director, or

(b)

the occurrence of any change in the particulars contained in its register of directors or its register of directors’ residential addresses,

(each a “filing deadline”) give notice to the Registrar of the change and of the date on which it occurred in a form that complies with the requirements of this section.

(1A) Where a company defaults in complying with subsection (1) by the relevant filing deadline, the company must pay a late filing fee up to the maximum amount prescribed in rules made by the Board

(1B) Where a delay in meeting the relevant filing deadline has resulted in a late filing fee being charged in accordance with subsection (1A) above, and the filing remains outstanding after the period for which a late filing fee can be charged pursuant to subsection (1A), the company has committed a contravention of subsection (1) and is liable to a fine up to level 4.

(2)

(a)
