INTRODUCTION

Application

This module (IFR) applies to:

(a)

every Person who carries on, or holds itself out as carrying on, a Financial Service in or from the DIFC as in accordance with Shari’a;

(b)

a Domestic Fund which is operated or held out as being operated as an Islamic Fund;

(c)

a Person making an Offer in or from the DIFC relating to an Investment or Crypto Token which is held out as Islamic or Shari’a compliant.
