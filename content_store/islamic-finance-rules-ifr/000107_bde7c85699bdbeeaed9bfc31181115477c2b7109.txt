Investments in other Funds

(1)

An Islamic Fund which is a Public Fund may invest in Units of another Fund only where the Fund Manager has taken reasonable care to determine that:

(a)

the other Fund is the subject of an independent annual audit conducted in accordance with relevant IFRS or other standards as applicable;

(b)

the other Fund has mechanisms in place to enable Unitholders to redeem their Units within a reasonable time; and

(c)

the other Fund is prohibited from having more than 20% of its value in the Units of Funds.

(2)

The Fund Manager must also have ascertained that there is a proper and disclosed basis for asset valuation and the pricing before investing in Units in the other Fund.
