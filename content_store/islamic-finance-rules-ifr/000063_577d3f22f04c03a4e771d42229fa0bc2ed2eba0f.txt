In this section:

(a)

“E” represents the Exposure determined by an Authorised Firm as applicable to an Islamic Contract; and

(b)

“CRW” represents the risk weighting or capital charge assessed by an Authorised Firm as appropriate to that Islamic Contract.

(2)

An Authorised Firm must determine the PSIACOMcredit an Islamic Contract by applying the following formula:

E x CRW x 8%.

(3)
