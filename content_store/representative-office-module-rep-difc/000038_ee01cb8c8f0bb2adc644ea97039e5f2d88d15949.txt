GENERAL PROVISIONS

4.1 General

4.1.1 A Representative Office must have a place of business within the

geographical boundaries of the DIFC.

4.1.2

(1)

A Representative Office must not:

(a)

share an office with another Authorised Firm, except as provided in (2);

(b)

represent anyone other than itself or a member of its Group; or

(c)

permit any staff member to be an Employee of another Authorised Person.

(2)

A Representative Office may share an office with another Authorised Firm that is a member of the same Group.
