REGULATORY PROCESSES

Notifications

A Representative Office must notify the DFSA as soon as reasonably practical of any change in its:

(a)

name;

(b)

legal status;

(c)

Controller(s); or

(d)

address.

A Representative Office must notify the DFSA as soon as reasonably practical of:

(a) any breach of a Rule or of a provision of DFSA-administered

legislation by the Representative Office; and

(b) any materially adverse information which would on reasonable grounds be considered likely to affect the fitness and propriety of the Representative Office or Principal Representative.
