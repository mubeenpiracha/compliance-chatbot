2.1 Licence application

2.1.1 A Person, referred to in this chapter as an applicant, who intends to carry on the Financial Service of Operating a Representative Office must apply to the DFSA for a Licence by completing and submitting the appropriate form in AFN.

2.1.2

(1)

An application to Operate a Representative Office may only be made by a Person who is:

(a)

incorporated; and

(b)

regulated by a Financial Services Regulator

in a jurisdiction other than the DIFC.

(2) The DFSA will not consider an application for a Licence from an applicant who intends to operate in the DIFC as a Domestic Firm.
