Suspension of Authorisation, Endorsement or Licence

(1)

The DFSA may suspend for a period not exceeding 12 months:

(a)

an authorisation in relation to one or more Financial Services for which an Authorised Person is authorised under its Licence;

(b)

a Licence Endorsement; or

(c)

a Licence of an Authorised Person.

(2)

The DFSA may act under this Article on its own initiative or at the request of an Authorised Person.
