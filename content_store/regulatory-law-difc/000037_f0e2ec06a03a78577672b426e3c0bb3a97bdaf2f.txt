PART 2: THE DFSA

CHAPTER 1 - THE STRUCTURE OF THE DFSA

7.

The DFSA

(1)

The DFSA is a body established under Dubai Law.

(2)

The DFSA shall have its own constitutional provisions, which shall incorporate the provisions of Schedule 2, and which shall not be inconsistent with those provisions.

(3)

In exercising its powers and performing its functions the DFSA shall act in an independent manner, notwithstanding that it is an agency of the government of the Emirate of Dubai.
