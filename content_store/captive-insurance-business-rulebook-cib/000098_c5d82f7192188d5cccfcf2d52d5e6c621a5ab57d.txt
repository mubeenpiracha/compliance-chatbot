8.2.2 Attribution of general Contracts of Insurance

(1)

Except as permitted by Rule 8.2.2(2), a Captive Insurer may not attribute general Contracts of Insurance to a Long-Term Insurance Fund.

(2)

A Captive Insurer may attribute Contracts of Insurance in General Insurance Class 1 or Class 2 to a Long-Term Insurance Fund.
