ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS FOR CELL COMPANIES

4.1

Definitions

For the purpose of Rule 4.1:

(a)

net Cellular Assets of a Captive Cell are the assets attributable to the Captive Cell (other than any deductible assets) less the liabilities attributable to the Captive Cell;

(b)

non-cellular eligible capital of a Captive Insurer that is a Cell Company means the Insurer's eligible capital calculated in accordance with Chapter 3, but excluding:

(a)

Cell Shares; and

(b)

any capital instruments or equity reserves that are attributable to a Captive Cell; and

(c)

recourse agreement means an agreement under which a Cell is entitled to have recourse to Non-Cellular Assets.
