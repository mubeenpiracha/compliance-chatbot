4.5.2

In this Rule, deductible assets of a Captive Cell means:

(a)

investments in Subsidiaries and Associates;

(b)

intangible assets; and

(c)

any other asset that the Regulator has directed the Captive Insurer to include under Rule 3.1.2(2)(b).
