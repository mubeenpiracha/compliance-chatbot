(3)

Notice of a Person having become a Company Service Provider of the DLT Foundation must be accompanied by that Person’s consent in the prescribed form to act in that capacity.

(4)

Prior to the registration of a DLT Foundation, the Founder or Founders may appoint a Company Service Provider by including the name and address in the ADGM of such Person in the Charter forming part of the DLT Foundation’s Registration Application.

A DLT Foundation may appoint and remove from office a Company Service Provider by Resolution of Councillors.

Obligations of Company Service Provider and DLT Foundation

The Company Service Provider of a DLT Foundation shall maintain the records that the DLT Foundation is required to maintain under or in accordance with any enactment or subordinate legislation applicable to the DLT Foundation, including:

(a)

these Regulations;

(b)

the Commercial Licensing Regulations 2015 (Conditions of Licence and Branch Registration) Rules 2023 and any licence granted thereunder; and

(c)

the Beneficial Ownership and Control Regulations 2022.

The Company Service Provider of a DLT Foundation:

(a)

must act as a Registered Office Provider to that DLT Foundation;

(b)

is authorised to represent the DLT Foundation in its dealings with the Registrar; and

(c)

shall give all notices and make all filings that the DLT Foundation is obliged to make to the Registrar under any enactment or subordinate legislation applicable to it, including:

i.

these Regulations;

ii.

the Commercial Licensing Regulations 2015 (Conditions of Licence and Branch Registration) Rules 2023 and any licence granted thereunder; and

iii.

the Beneficial Ownership and Control Regulations 2022.

13.

Service of Documents on DLT Foundations

A document may be served on a DLT Foundation registered under these Regulations by leaving it at, or sending it by post or courier to, the DLT Foundation’s registered office, or the office of its Company Service Provider appointed in accordance with these Regulations.
