Owner's Equity

In relation to:

(a)

a Takaful Provider, means the amount of the assets, less the liabilities, of the Insurer that are not attributed to the Takaful Fund of the Takaful Provider; and

(b)

an Authorised Person that manages a PSIA, means the amount of the assets, less the liabilities, of that Authorised Person that are not attributed to any PSIA managed by that Authorised Person.

Parent

Means a Holding Company as defined in section 1015 of the Companies Regulations.

Parent Undertaking

Has the meaning given to that term in the Companies Regulations.
