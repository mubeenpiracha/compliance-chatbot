Mining Tenement

Model Portfolio

Means a portfolio of segregated holdings managed on a discretionary basis by an Investment Manager where all Clients in a particular profile or model receive the same portfolios and are traded and adjusted at the same time and in the same way, but with each Client having a separate Discretionary Portfolio Management Agreement in place with the relevant Investment Manager.
