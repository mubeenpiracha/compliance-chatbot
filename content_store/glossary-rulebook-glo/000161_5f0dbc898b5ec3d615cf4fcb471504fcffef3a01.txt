Definitions

Means all quantities of Petroleum (recoverable and unrecoverable) naturally occurring on or within the Earth’s crust, discovered and undiscovered, plus those quantities already produced. It includes all types of Petroleum whether currently considered ‘conventional’ or ‘unconventional’.
