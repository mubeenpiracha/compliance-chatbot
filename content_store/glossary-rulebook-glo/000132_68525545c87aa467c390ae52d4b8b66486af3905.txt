Means in relation to an Investment, a Person who holds himself out as able and willing to enter into transactions of sale and purchase in Investments of that description at prices determined by him generally and continuously rather than in respect of each particular transaction.

Definitions

Means the behaviour specified in section 92(4) of FSMR.

Means:

(a)

in relation to COBS, any material communicated to a Person in the course of marketing Regulated Activities or Specified Investments or effecting introductions; and

(b)

in relation to GEN 9.12 and 9.13, any material communicated to a Person in the course of marketing financial services or financial products or effecting introductions.
