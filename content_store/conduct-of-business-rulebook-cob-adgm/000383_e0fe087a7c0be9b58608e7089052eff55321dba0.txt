Information required prior to the conclusion of a Single Payment Service Contract

19.4.1 A Payment Service Provider must provide or make available to a Payment Service User the following information in relation to the Payment Service, whether by supplying a copy of the draft Single Payment Service Contract or a copy of the draft Payment Order or otherwise, before the Payment Service User is bound by the Single Payment Service Contract:

(a)

the information or unique identifier that has to be provided by the Payment Service User in order for a Payment Order to be properly initiated or executed;

(b)

the maximum time in which the Payment Service will be executed;
