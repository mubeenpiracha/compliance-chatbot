3.8.17 The Authorised Person acting as the Investment Manager of an ADGM Climate Transition Portfolio must provide a copy of the attestation obtained for the purposes of Rule 3.8.14 to each Client with whom it has entered into a Discretionary Portfolio Management Agreement in respect of such ADGM Climate Transition Portfolio at least on an annual basis and upon request by the Client.

3.8.18 Prior to entering into a Discretionary Portfolio Management Agreement with a Retail

Client in relation to an ADGM Climate Transition Portfolio, an Authorised Person must:

(a)

provide written disclosure to the Client how it assesses, measures and monitors compliance with the Climate Transition Portfolio Investment Requirement; and

(b)

at least annually, disclose the progress of the ADGM Climate Transition Portfolio in meeting its expressed climate transition objectives.
