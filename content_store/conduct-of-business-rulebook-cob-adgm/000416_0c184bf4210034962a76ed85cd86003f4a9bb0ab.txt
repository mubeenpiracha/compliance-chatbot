A Payment Service Provider which issues Stored Value must not award:

(a)

interest in respect of the holding of Stored Value; or

(b)

any other benefit, monetary or otherwise, related to the length of time during which an Payment Service User holds Stored Value.

(8)

For the purposes of this Rule, a Framework Contract between a Payment Service Provider and a Payment Service User terminates when the right to use Stored Value for the purpose of making Payment Transactions ceases.
