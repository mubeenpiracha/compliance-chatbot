17.7.4 An Authorised Person that is Operating a Multilateral Trading Facility in relation to Virtual Assets must comply with the following requirements set out in MIR, Chapter 5:

(a)

Rules 5.1 - 5.3; and

(b)

Rule 5.4.1, in the circumstances identified in Items 19, 20, 24 (a) and (b), 27, 28, 32, 33, 35, 37, 38, 39, 40, 41, 43, 45, 46, 47, 48, 49, 50, 52, 53, 54, 55, 57, 58, 59, 60, 61 and 62.

17.7.5 For the purposes of Rule 17.7.4, all references in MIR to:

(a)

“Recognised Body” or “Recognised Bodies” shall be read as references to “Authorised Person”; and

(b)

“Financial Instrument” or “Financial Instruments” shall be read as references to “Virtual Asset” or “Virtual Assets”, as applicable.

17.7.6 GEN Rule 5.2.14 shall apply to an Authorised Person Operating a Multilateral Trading Facility in relation to Virtual Assets, and all references to “Investment” shall be read as references to “Virtual Asset”.
