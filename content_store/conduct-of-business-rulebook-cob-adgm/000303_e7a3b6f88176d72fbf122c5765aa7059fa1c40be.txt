15.5.2 An Authorised Person must be able to demonstrate to the Regulator's satisfaction the grounds upon which the Authorised Person considers the Third-Party Agent to be suitable to hold Safe Custody Assets.

15.5.3 For the purposes of a Client Account established in accordance with the Safe Custody Rules, a Third-Party Agent is a Financial Institution which may be a bank, custodian, an intermediate broker, a settlement agent, a clearing house, an exchange or an “over-the- counter” counterparty acting in the capacity of third-party agent.
