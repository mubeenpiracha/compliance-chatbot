Providing Credit

4.3.1 An Authorised Person may Provide Credit to a Professional Client.

4.3.2 An Authorised Person may Provide Credit to a Retail Client only where:

(a)

the Retail Client is an Undertaking; and

(b)

the Credit Facility is provided to the Retail Client for a business purpose.
