102. Resignation of an auditor

(1)

In this Article, a Relevant Person means an Authorised Person, Public Listed Company or Domestic Fund.

(2)

An Auditor of a Relevant Person may resign as Auditor by depositing a notice in writing to that effect together with a statement referred to in Articles 102(3) and (4), at the registered office of the Relevant Person and any such notice operates
