Auditor

has the meaning given to that term in Article 97(a) of this Law.

Audit Principal

has the meaning given to that term in Article 97(c) of this Law.

Affiliate

in relation to an Authorised Person, means any other entity in the Group to which the Authorised Person belongs.

Authorised Firm

a person who holds a Licence to carry on one or more Financial Services prescribed pursuant to Article 42(1)(a).

Authorised Individual

an individual who has been authorised by the DFSA to perform one or more Licensed Functions for an Authorised Firm.
