(3)

The DFSA may vary or withdraw a restriction imposed under this Article.

(4)

A person who performs a function in breach of a restriction under this Article commits a contravention.

(5)

The procedures in Schedule 3 apply to a decision of the DFSA under Article 59(1).

(6)

If the DFSA decides to exercise its power under Article 59(1), the person may refer the matter to the FMT for review.
