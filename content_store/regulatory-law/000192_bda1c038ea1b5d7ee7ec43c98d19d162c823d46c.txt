84F. Resolvability Assessment

(1)

The DFSA may conduct an assessment to determine if there are any impediments that may prevent or affect the Resolvability of an Authorised Firm (the “Resolvability Assessment”).

(2)

An Authorised Firm must provide the DFSA with such information and assistance that the DFSA considers reasonably necessary for the Resolvability Assessment.
