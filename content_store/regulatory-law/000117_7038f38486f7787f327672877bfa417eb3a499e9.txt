CHAPTER 5 - AUTHORISED INDIVIDUAL AND KEY INDIVIDUAL STATUS

53. Applications for Authorised Individual or Key Individual status

(1)

In this Chapter:

(a)

"Authorised Person” includes a person whose application for a Licence or application for extension of a Licence is being considered by the DFSA; and

(b)

“director, officer, employee or agent” of a person includes a person who proposes to become a director, officer, employee or agent of a person.
