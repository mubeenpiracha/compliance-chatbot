APP3 CONTRACT DELIVERY SPECIFICATIONS

A3.1 Application

A3.1.1 This Appendix applies to an Authorised Market Institution which trades, or clears or settles, on its facilities Commodity Derivative contracts which require physical delivery of the underlying commodity.
