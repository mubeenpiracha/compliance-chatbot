(2)

For the purposes of (1), an Exchange must have adequate powers over its Members to address risks to an orderly functioning of its facilities arising from unsettled positions in Investments.

(3)

Short Selling for the purposes of this Rule constitutes the sale of a Security by a Person who does not own the Security at the point of entering into the contract to sell.
