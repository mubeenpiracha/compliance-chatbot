Units in collective investment funds

When assessing whether Units are capable of being traded in a fair, orderly and efficient manner for the purposes of Rule 3.9.1, a Recognised Investment Exchange shall take the following aspects into account:

(a)

For an open-ended Collective Investment Fund:

the distribution of those Units to the public;

whether there are appropriate market-making arrangements, or the Fund Manager provides appropriate alternative whether arrangements for investors to redeem the Units; and

whether the value of the units is made sufficiently transparent to investors by means of the periodic publication of the net asset value.
