15.2 General Requirements

15.2.1 The provisions of this chapter are referred to as the Safe Custody Rules.

15.2.2 An Authorised Person must:

(a)

comply with the Safe Custody Rules; and

(b)

have adequate systems and controls in place to be able to evidence compliance with the Safe Custody Rules.
