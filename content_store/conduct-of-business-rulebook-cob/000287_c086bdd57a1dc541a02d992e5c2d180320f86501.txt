14.13 Client Money Distribution Rules

14.13.1 To the extent that the rules in this section ("the Client Money Distribution Rules") are

inconsistent with section 225 of the Insolvency Regulations, these Rules will prevail.

14.13.2 If an Authorised Person becomes insolvent, or a Pooling Event Occurs, the available

funds in each Client Account must be distributed in accordance with Rule 14.13.4.
