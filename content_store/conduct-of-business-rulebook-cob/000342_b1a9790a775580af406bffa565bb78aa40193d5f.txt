International Tax Reporting Obligations

17.4.1 An Authorised Person conducting a Regulated Activity in relation to Virtual Assets or Fiat- Referenced Tokens, where applicable, must comply with all reporting obligations in relation to-

(a)

FATCA, as set out in the Foreign Account Tax Compliance Regulations 2022; and

(b)

Common Reporting Standards, set out in the ADGM Common Reporting Standard Regulations 2017.
