Systems and Controls

22.9.2 An Authorised Person must have adequate arrangements demonstrating that it:

(a)

can operate a Spot Commodity Auction Platform;

(b)

can assess, mitigate and manage the risks relating to the performance of a Spot Commodity Auction Platform;

(c)

monitors bids made by, and transactions effected by, participants on the Spot Commodity Auction Platform;

(d)

is responsible for, and performs, the full technical operation of the Spot Commodity Auction Platform, including in relation to contingency arrangements for disruption to the Spot Commodity Auction Platform; and

(e)

is responsible for, and operates, the arrangements set out in Rule 22.9.3.
