15.4.4 An Authorised Person must not use a Client's Safe Custody Assets for its own purpose or

15.4.5 An Authorised Person which intends to use a Client's Safe Custody Assets for its own purpose or that of another Person, must have systems and controls in place to ensure that:

(a)

it obtains that Client's prior written permission;

(b)

adequate records are maintained to protect Safe Custody Assets which are applied as collateral or used for stock lending activities;

(c)

the equivalent assets are returned to the Client Account of the Client; and

(d)

the Client is not disadvantaged by the use of his Safe Custody Assets.
