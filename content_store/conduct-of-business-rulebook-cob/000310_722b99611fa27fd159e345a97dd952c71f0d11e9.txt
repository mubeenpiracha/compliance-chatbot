15.8 Client Reporting

15.8.1 An Authorised Person which provides Custody or which otherwise holds or controls any

Safe Custody Assets for a Client must send a regular statement to its Client:

(a)

if it is a Retail Client at least every six months; or

(b)

if it is a Professional Client at other intervals as agreed in writing with the Professional Client.

15.8.2 The statement must include:

(a)

a list of that Client's Safe Custody Assets as at the date of reporting; and

(b)

a list of that Client's Collateral and the market value of that Collateral as at the date of reporting.
