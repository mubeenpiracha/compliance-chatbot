A1.1.2

In producing a Regulatory Returns Auditor’s Report for a Branch, an Auditor must state whether;

(a)

the Authorised Person’s regulatory returns have been properly reconciled with the Branch trial balance; and

(b)

in the case of an Authorised Firm;

(i)

the regulatory returns specified in PIN or PIB have been properly prepared by the Authorised Firm in accordance with the applicable rules in PIB or PIN; and

(ii)

the Authorised Firm has kept proper Accounting Records in accordance with the applicable Rules in PIB or PIN.
