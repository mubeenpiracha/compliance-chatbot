Capital requirements

4.2.1

A Recognised Clearing House shall hold capital more than or equal to the sum of capital calculated in respect of the following risks.

(a) Winding down or restructuring activities. Six months' gross operating

expenses.

(b)

Operational risks. A Recognised Clearing House shall calculate its capital requirement for operational risks using either the Basic Indicator Approach or, with prior authorisation from the Regulator, the Standardised Approach or the Alternative Standardised Approach, both as provided specifically in Appendix 7.3 and generally in Appendix 7 of PRU.
