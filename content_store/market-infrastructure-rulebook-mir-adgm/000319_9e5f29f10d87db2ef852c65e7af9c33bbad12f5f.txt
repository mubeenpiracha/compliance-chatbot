Remote Member Supervision

Guidance

The Regulator will rely upon a Remote Member’s Non-ADGM Financial Services Regulator in its home jurisdiction to supervise the Remote Member. The focus of the Regulator’s interest will be on ADGM and those activities of the Remote Member that may impact ADGM.
