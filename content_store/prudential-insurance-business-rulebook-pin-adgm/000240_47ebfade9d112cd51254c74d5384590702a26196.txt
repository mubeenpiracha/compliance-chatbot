A4.2 Minimum Capital Requirement

A4.2.1 Subject to Rule A4.2.2, an Insurer must calculate its Minimum Capital Requirement

according to the formula:

MCR = DRC + IVRC + OARC + OLRC + CRC + SFAC + URC + RRC + LIRC + AMRC

Where the following definitions apply:

Term

Definition

MCR

Insurer's Minimum Capital Requirement;

DRC

Insurer's default risk component;

IVRC

Insurer's investment volatility risk component;

OARC

Insurer's off-balance sheet asset risk component;

OLRC

Insurer's off-balance sheet liability risk component;
