9.7.3

A Fund Manager and an Authorised Person making an Offer of a Unit of a Fund meet the requirement in Rule 9.7.1 or Rule 9.7.2 as is relevant by:

(a)

maintaining at its place of business in ADGM copies of the relevant Prospectus for inspection by Clients and by the Regulator during normal business hours; or

(b)

being able to advise readily of a location in ADGM where copies of the Prospectus are available.
