20.4.6

Upon granting a Fund an ADGM Green Fund Designation or ADGM Climate Transition Fund Designation as applicable where applied for under Rule 20.4.5, the Regulator will publish the name and details of the Fund on the list of ADGM Green Funds and ADGM Climate Transition Funds maintained by the Regulator on its website.
