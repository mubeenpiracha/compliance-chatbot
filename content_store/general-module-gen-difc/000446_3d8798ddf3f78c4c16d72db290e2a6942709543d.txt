Lead regulation

11.1.5 (1)

If requested by the DFSA, an Authorised Person must provide the DFSA with information that the Authorised Person or its auditor has provided to a Financial Services Regulator.

(2)

If requested by take reasonable steps to provide the DFSA with information that other members of the Authorised Person’s Group have provided to a Financial Services Regulator.
