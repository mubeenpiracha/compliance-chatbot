Financial instrument declared as an investment

(1)

The DFSA may, subject to (5), declare by written notice any financial instrument or class of financial instruments to be a particular type of an existing Security or Derivative as defined in these Rules or a new type of a Security or Derivative. It may do so on such terms and conditions as it considers appropriate.

(2)

The DFSA may exercise the power under (1) either upon written application made by a Person or on its own initiative.
