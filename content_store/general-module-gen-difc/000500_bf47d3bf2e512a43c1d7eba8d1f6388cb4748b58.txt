Winding up, bankruptcy and insolvency

11.10.16 An Authorised Person must notify the DFSA immediately on:

(a)

the calling of a meeting to consider a resolution for winding up the Authorised Person;

(b)

an application to dissolve the Authorised Person or to strike it from the register maintained by the DIFC Registrar of Companies, or a comparable register in another jurisdiction;

(c)

the presentation of a petition for the winding up of the Authorised Person;
