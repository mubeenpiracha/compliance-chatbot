GENERAL (GEN)

Advising on Financial Products or Credit

10.3.5 An Authorised Firm which, immediately before the commencement date, had an authorisation for ‘Advising on Financial Products or Credit’ in respect of Financial Products and credit is, on the commencement date, taken to have the following authorisations:

(a)

‘Arranging Credit and Advising on Credit’ so far as that authorisation relates to Advising on Credit; and

(b)

‘Advising on Financial Products’, but only with respect to Financial Products the its Licence commencement date.
