APP4 CONTRACTS OF INSURANCE

A4.1

Definition of a contract of insurance

A4.1.1

A Contract of Insurance means any contract of insurance or contract of reinsurance.

A4.1.2

The classes of life insurance are as follows:

Class I – Life and annuity

(a)

Contracts of insurance on human life or contracts to pay annuities on human life, but excluding, in each case, contracts within (c).

Class II – Marriage and birth

(b)

Contracts of insurance to provide a sum on marriage or on the birth of a child, being contracts expressed to be in effect for a period of more than one year.
