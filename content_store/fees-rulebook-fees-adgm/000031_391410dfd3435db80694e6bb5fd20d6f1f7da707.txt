3.13 Operating a Representative Office

3.13.1 An Applicant for a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Operating a Representative Office must pay to the Regulator an application fee of $5,000.

3.13.2 An Authorised Person with a Financial Services Permission to carry on the Regulated Activity of Operating a Representative Office must pay to the Regulator an annual supervision fee of $5,000.
