Admission to the Official List of Securities

9.2.1 An Applicant for admission of Securities to the Official List of Securities must pay to the

Regulator an application fee of $3,000.

Guidance

A Listed Entity with Securities admitted to the Official List of Securities is not required to pay an annual supervision fee to the Regulator.

9.3

Approval of an Offer document originating from outside ADGM

9.3.1 A Person applying to the Regulator for approval of an Offer document produced under legislation in a jurisdiction other than ADGM must pay to the Regulator a filing fee of $5,000.
