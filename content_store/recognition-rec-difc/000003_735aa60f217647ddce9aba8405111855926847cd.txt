APPLICATION OF THE RECOGNITION MODULE

1.1 Application

1.1.1 This module (REC) applies to every Recognised Person and to applicants for admission to the list of Recognised Persons under Part 3 of the Markets Law 2012.

Guidance

1.

A Recognised Person is defined in Article 37(3) as a Recognised body or a Recognised Member. A Recognised Body and a Recognised Member are also defined in Article 37(3) as follows:

a.

“recognised body” means a person which operates an exchange, clearing house or alternative trading system in a jurisdiction other than the DIFC which has been admitted to, and appears on, the list of recognised bodies maintained by the DFSA pursuant to this Article.
