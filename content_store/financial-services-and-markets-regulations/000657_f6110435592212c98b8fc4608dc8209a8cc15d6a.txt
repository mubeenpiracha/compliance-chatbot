Exclusions

A person ("A") licensed under the Commercial Licensing Regulations 2015 to carry on the activities specified in Rules 4 or 5 of the Commercial Licensing Regulations 2015 (Controlled Activities) Rules 2015 does not Provide Trust Services where it only—

(a)

arranges for a person to act as trustee in respect of an express trust; or

(b)

provides services with respect to the creation of an express trust;
