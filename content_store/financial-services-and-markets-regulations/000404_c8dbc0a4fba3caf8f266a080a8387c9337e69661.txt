Gaming contracts

223. Gaming contracts

(1)

No contract to which this section applies is void or unenforceable because of any rule of law or any enactment relating to gaming or wagering contracts.

(2)

This section applies to a contract if it is entered into by either or each party in the course of carrying on Regulated Activities.
