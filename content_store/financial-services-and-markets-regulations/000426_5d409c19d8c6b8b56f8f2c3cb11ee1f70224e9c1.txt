The power of the Court to grant an injunction restraining a person ("A") from engaging in conduct under subsection (2) may be exercised—

(a)

whether or not it appears to the Court that A intends to engage again, or to continue to engage, in conduct of that kind;

(b)

whether or not A has previously engaged in conduct of that kind; or

(c)

whether or not there is an imminent danger of substantial damage to any person if A engages in conduct of that kind.
