Assets Requirement20

(1)

This section applies if—

(a)

the Regulator imposes an Assets Requirement on a person being given a Financial Services Permission;

(b)

an Assets Requirement is imposed on an Authorised Person; or

(c)

an Assets Requirement previously imposed on such a person is varied.

(2)

A person on whom an Assets Requirement is imposed is referred to in this section as "A".

(3)

"Assets Requirement" means a requirement imposed under section 35—

(a)
