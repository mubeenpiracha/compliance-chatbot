124A. Imposition of Requirements by the Regulator

(1)

Where a person has applied to the Regulator for a Recognition Order, or a Recognised Body, Remote Body or Remote Member has applied to the Regulator for the variation of a Recognition Order previously granted, the Regulator may impose on that person such Requirements, taking effect on or after the giving or variation of the Recognition Order, as the Regulator considers appropriate.
