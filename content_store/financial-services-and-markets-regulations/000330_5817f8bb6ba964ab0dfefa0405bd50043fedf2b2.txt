Part 14 Suspension and Removal of Financial Instruments from Trading

180. Regulator's power to require suspension or removal of Financial Instruments from Trading

The Regulator may, for the purpose of protecting—

(a)

the interests of investors; or

(b)

the orderly functioning of the financial markets;

require an Institution or a class of Institutions to suspend or remove a Financial Instrument from Trading.

181. Suspension or removal of Financial Instruments from trading: procedure130

(1)

A requirement imposed under section 180 (a "Section 180 Requirement") takes effect—

(a)

immediately, if the notice given under subsection (2) states that this is the case;

(b)

in any other case, on such date as may be specified in the notice.
