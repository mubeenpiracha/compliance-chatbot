Qualifying Person

means—

(a)

for the purposes of section 239, a person appearing to the Court to be someone—

(i)

to whom the profits mentioned in section 239(1)(a) are attributable; or

(ii)

who has suffered the loss or adverse effect mentioned in section 239(1)(b); and

(b)

for the purposes of section 240, a person appearing to the Court to be someone—

(i)

to whom the profits mentioned in section 240(2)(a) are attributable; or

(ii)

who has suffered the loss or adverse effect mentioned in section 240(2)(b).
