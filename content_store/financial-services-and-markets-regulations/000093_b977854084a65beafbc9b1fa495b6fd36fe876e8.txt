Part 6 Official Listing and Offers

The Official List

50.

The Official List

(1)

The Regulator must maintain the Official List. In maintaining the Official List the Regulator may refer to itself as the Listing Authority.25

(2)

The Regulator may admit to the Official List such Securities as it considers appropriate in accordance with this Part.

(3)

A Recognised Investment Exchange shall not permit trading of Securities on its facilities unless those Securities are admitted to, and not suspended from, the Official List except where otherwise prescribed in the Rules made by the Regulator.
