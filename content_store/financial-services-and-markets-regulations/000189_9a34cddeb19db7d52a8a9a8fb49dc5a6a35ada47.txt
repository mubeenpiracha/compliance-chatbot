123. Applications: supplementary

(1)

At any time after receiving an Application and before determining it, the Regulator may require the Applicant to provide such further information as it reasonably considers necessary to enable it to determine the Application.

(2)

Information which the Regulator requires in connection with an Application must be provided in such form, or verified in such manner, as the Regulator may direct.

(3)

Different directions may be given, or requirements imposed, by the Regulator with respect to different Applications.
